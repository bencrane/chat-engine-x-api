# Lob Credits

## What are Lob Credits?

* Lob Credits have a 1:1 USD value and can be used for print and mail usage and related taxes.
* Fund your Lob Credits as needed (or automate the process) to ensure you have sufficient funds prior to sending mail.
* Have visibility and control over your Lob Credits balance in the Lob dashboard.&#x20;

We are committed to optimizing your direct mail experience and serving you within the most ideal framework. With Lob Credits, you’ll have full control over your expenses, can plan and budget more effectively, and can benefit from greater financial predictability.

The following is an overview we encourage you to review with your Accounts Payable and Finance departments to understand how to fund your mail sends with Lob Credits.&#x20;

## How do I fund my Lob Credits?

A customer will fund their Lob Credits differently depending on what payment process/method they prefer, but they can do it ad-hoc, or take advantage of available automation to support ongoing funding. Select the situation that applies to you to learn more.&#x20;

#### Set up Auto Pay <a href="#set-up-lc-auto-pay" id="set-up-lc-auto-pay"></a>

You can automate payment to ensure funds are always available when you need to send mail.

There are two types of [Auto Pay - Recurring](#auto-pay-recurring) (based on time) and [Auto Pay - Threshold](#auto-pay-threshold) (triggered from user-defined Lob Credit balance).

#### **Auto Pay - Recurring**

1. From the Add Lob Credits screen, select Funding Type: Recurring
2. Fill in:

* Name
* Cadence
  * Weekly: Initiates payment for the user-defined amount on the 1st, 8th, 15th, and 22nd of the month&#x20;
  * Monthly:  Initiates payment on the 1st of each month
  * Quarterly:  Initiates payment  on the 1st day of each fiscal quarter (Jan 1, Apr 1, July 1, Oct 1)
* Amount

<img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/So2x1eiquGnBZKnd86xBYj9nzyROlGA8_0vBqc6nzb1tUIAmW0mjz527Gcia78Z32jSbFPLkKXosOtboJLRmreGYTEbPsKAJJllIuSIt9aHJFJpl5jdWO52_yBKZhhlYJmzpJ-KbvaT7Ju5Zm1aS4VhK7Q=nw" alt="Funding type: Recurring Schedule" data-size="original">

3. Payment Method is separate from Funding Type; after you up the trigger, you can select payment method.
4. Confirm action.

#### **Auto Pay - Threshold**

1. From the Add Lob Credits screen, select Funding Type: Threshold
2. Fill in:

* Name
* Threshold Amount: When Lob Credits balance drops below this amount, payment will be triggered (your Lob Credit balance must be greater than the Threshold Amount at the time you are setting the threshold)
* Refill Amount: When a payment is triggered, it will be for this amount

4. Confirm action.

## How soon will I be able to use my Lob Credits?

{% hint style="warning" %}
You must have sufficient Lob Credits to send mail; therefore, it is essential to understand the process and timing of funding your Lob Credits.
{% endhint %}

Lob Credits will be available when funds are fully settled in Lob Bank accounts; you will receive an email notification when this occurs and Lob Credits are available for use. Lob will continue to accept multiple forms of payment methods; reference [Lob Payment Methods ](https://help.lob.com/account-management/billing/lob-payment-methods)for more information on this and funds availability.&#x20;

* **For credit card,** Lob Credits will typically be available immediately upon successful charge.
* **For all other payment methods**, timing will depend on your Accounts Payable processes, as well as payment method and funds availability.&#x20;

All invoices and their statuses will still be available under the Billing > Invoices tab.

## Will I be notified when I run out of Lob Credits?

{% hint style="success" %}
Yes! Regardless of how you send direct mail with Lob, all users will be notified when your Lob Credit balance reaches zero via email and in the dashboard.&#x20;
{% endhint %}

Additionally,

* **Print & Mail API:** You will receive a 200 OK API response even if Lob Credits balance reaches zero. After 5/1, if Lob Credits balance is below zero, mailpieces will be placed into “[Funding Hold.](#what-if-i-do-not-have-enough-lob-credits-for-my-campaign)”&#x20;
* **Campaigns API:** You will receive a 200 OK API response even if their Lob Credits balance reaches zero. After 5/1, you will receive a 422 error response if you do not have enough Lob Credits. Mailpieces will be placed into “[Funding Hold](#what-if-i-do-not-have-enough-lob-credits-for-my-campaign).”&#x20;
* **Campaigns tool:** You will be presented with an estimated cost of your Campaign and can place the order. Beginning 5/1:

  * A pop-up will be presented if you do not have sufficient Lob Credits
  * You will be directed to Add Funds & Place Order.&#x20;
  * You will be able to add funds almost immediately (with a credit card on file) OR be directed to Billing to fund your Lob Credits.&#x20;
    * In either case, your Campaign will be saved as a Draft

## Funding Hold

If you do not have enough Lob Credits to send mail, i.e., when Lob estimates there are insufficient funds to send mailpieces, they will go into *Funding Hold* status. **Mailpieces will still be created, but not sent.**

You can view the total dollar amount of mailpieces in Funding Hold on the Lob Credits home screen.

<figure><img src="https://1775164782-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FiXDYGxBp6aAwhpEhTMNC%2Fuploads%2FyPy6D0JWDHJabVpS7Uld%2Ffunding_hold_home.png?alt=media&#x26;token=9e01dea9-dd08-4f8f-b0a9-4e8b93e6021c" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once Lob receives payment, we will re-evaluate all mailpieces in Funding Hold and release the paid mailpieces to a printer. Mailpieces will be released first in, first out (FIFO) based on Date Created.&#x20;

If your payment does not cover all mailpieces in Funding Hold, those that are not paid will re-enter Funding Hold.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Canceling mailpieces in Funding Hold**

This functionality is on our roadmap; if you need to cancel mailpieces in Funding Hold, please contact [support@lob.com.](mailto:support@lob.com)
{% endhint %}

## Other FAQs

<details>

<summary>Do Lob Credits expire?</summary>

Lob Credits will not expire. Any amounts due at the end of the contract or termination of the customer relationship will be refunded, after final reconciliation.

</details>

<details>

<summary>I already paid a Prefund Amount, what will happen to that?</summary>

At launch, your Prefund Amount balance will automatically be converted to your Lob Credits Beginning Balance. All mail usage ordered and sent from that date forward will be deducted from your Lob Credits balance.

You may not need to take immediate action upon launch as you will likley have Lob Credits to draw from, but we do recommend understanding the process (and timing) around funding your Lob Credits, so you can replenish in advance of sending mail.&#x20;

</details>

<details>

<summary>My company requires every invoice to be tied to a specific Purchase Order, how will this work if Lob is not issuing usage invoices any longer?</summary>

When you create a Lob Credits [invoice-on-demand](#i-do-not-have-a-payment-method-on-file-with-lob-i-pay-via-invoice) you can input a Purchase Order number (optional field); your Accounts Payable department will then be able to match the invoice to the approved PO.

</details>

<details>

<summary>My company issues monthly or quarterly budgets, how will I manage my Lob Credits with my budget?</summary>

However you fund your Lob Credits, there is an option to set up automation so you can ensure funds are always available when you need to send mail.

* [If you have a payment method on file with Lob,](#i-have-a-payment-method-on-file-with-lob) you can set up autopay to occur on a set cadence, or at a certain threshold.
* [If you DO NOT have a payment method on file with Lob,](#i-do-not-have-a-payment-method-on-file-with-lob) you can set up recurring invoice-on-demand generation (weekly, monthly, quarterly, etc.) for Lob Credits&#x20;

You will need to keep in mind that any ad-hoc, high-volume campaigns will need advance financial planning to ensure sufficient Lob Credits are available in your account before campaign execution.

To ensure there are sufficient funds to send mail when needed, it is recommended that you have at minimum quarterly budgets/PO set up and approved (vs. monthly or less).

</details>

<details>

<summary>How do Threshold payments work? What if I encounter a funding hold?</summary>

Shortly after sending mail, and additionally once each evening, Lob's system will automatically check to see if your threshold amount has been surpassed. If it has, an invoice will be generated for the refill amount specified in your threshold settings.

If your refill amount is insufficient to clear your funding hold, but surpasses your threshold amount, Lob will continue to push mail out of your funding hold until your Lob Credits balance is depleted. Such scenarios will cause the threshold to trigger daily until the funding hold has been cleared. Note that customers on invoice-only payment terms (see [here](#i-do-not-have-a-payment-method-on-file-with-lob-i-pay-via-invoice)) will not automatically have their invoices charged, and payment will need to be pushed to Lob in order for the funding hold to clear.

</details>

<details>

<summary>I send mail irregularly, or on a quarterly or annual basis; do I need to maintain a minimum balance at all times?</summary>

No! You can manage your Lob Credits any way you would like, as long as there are sufficient funds before you send mail.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Will I be notified when I am about to exceed my mailing limits, which have various pricing tiers and overage fees?</summary>

Your Account Manager will closely monitor your subscription and mailing limits, and let you know proactively when you are reaching your limit. We plan on building features to improve this experience after our initial launch.

</details>

<details>

<summary>I need to manage multiple Billing Groups separately as each department has a different budget; how will this work?</summary>

Billing Group is not currently supported. In a future release (target Q3), you will have the ability to allocate Lob Credits to one or many Billing Groups.&#x20;

</details>

<details>

<summary>I have multiple Billing Groups, but I want to manage my Lob Credits at the account level, not at a Billing Group level; how will this work?</summary>

Billing Group is not currently supported. When the ability to support Billing Groups has launched (target Q3), you will be able to manage Lob Credits at the account level. Usage will be deducted from your Lob Credits on a “first in, first out” basis.&#x20;

</details>
