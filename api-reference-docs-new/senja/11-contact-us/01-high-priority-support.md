# Do you offer high-priority or 1:1 support?

This article explains our support policy regarding priority and one-on-one assistance.

Do we offer high-priority or 1:1 support?

We do not offer high-priority or one-on-one support.

We aim to provide equitable service to all our customers.

What is your support response time?

We strive to address every support ticket promptly.

Our goal is to resolve all tickets within two days or less.

This applies to all customers.
