# Does Senja offer live demos or Zoom calls?

Thanks for your interest in Senja!

We're a small, dedicated team passionate about helping you succeed.

To stay focused on building the best product and providing top-notch support, we don't offer live one-on-one demos or Zoom calls.

Instead, we work asynchronously and have created a wealth of resources to help you get started and answer your questions.

What are the alternatives to a live demo?

We offer several ways for you to learn about Senja and get the help you need:📹 Watch our comprehensive video demoWe’ve created a detailed 14-minute video walkthrough that covers everything you'd learn in a live demo, and more.

It’s the best way to get a feel for Senja and see how it can work for you.📆 Join our monthly Office HoursEvery month, we host a live Office Hours session where you can learn about new features, ask questions, and hear from other Senja users.

It's a great way to get your questions answered in a group setting.RSVP for an upcoming session on Luma.

Can't make it live?

All Office Hours sessions are recorded and posted to the Senja YouTube page so you can watch the replay anytime.🎥 Get personalized async video supportHave specific questions after watching the demo?

We’d love to help!

Record a quick Loom video with your questions, send it to our support team, and we'll get back to you with a personalized video response.

This is often the most effective way to get detailed answers to your specific questions, and you can rewatch the video anytime!📚 Read our Getting Started guideOur How do I get started with Senja article is a step-by-step guide to get you up and running in no time.

Why this async approach is better for youFaster responses: No need to coordinate schedules for a meeting.

More detailed answers: We can take the time to create a thorough response for you.

You have a record: You can always refer back to video responses and written guides.

Available 24/7: Our resources are available whenever you need them.
