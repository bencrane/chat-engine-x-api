# Can I migrate to Senja from Testimonial.to?

You can migrate from Testimonial.to to Senja with one click. We offer three migration methods depending on what testimonials you want to import. You'll need a Senja account first. Sign up here if you haven't already.

## Option 1: Import from Wall of Love (recommended)

This is the fastest method for importing both text and video testimonials.

**Benefits:**
- Import text and video testimonials with a single URL
- One-click process

**Drawback:**
- Only imports testimonials that are on your Testimonial.to Wall of Love

**Steps:**

1. Open Senja and click Import
2. Click Migrate
3. Add your Testimonial.to Wall of Love URL
4. Click Import testimonials

Your testimonials will import immediately and appear in your Senja dashboard.

## Option 2: Import using CSV export

Use this method if you need to import unapproved or private testimonials.

**Benefits:**
- Import public and unapproved text and video testimonials

**Drawback:**
- Social posts won't be included (Testimonial.to doesn't export them)

**Steps:**

1. Export your testimonials from Testimonial.to (export all as CSV)
2. Add testimonials to your Wall of Love. Before exporting, make sure all the testimonials you want to migrate are added to your Wall of Love in Testimonial.to
3. Contact Senja support. Email us at support@senja.io with:
   - Your Senja account email
   - Your exported CSV file attached

We typically complete CSV imports within 24 hours.

## Option 3: Manual import

If you prefer to import testimonials yourself, navigate to Import in your Senja dashboard.

You can add testimonials individually or use bulk import. Learn more about bulk imports here.
