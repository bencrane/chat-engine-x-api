# How to find and manage my testimonials

All your testimonials live in the Proof page of your Senja dashboard, where you can view, filter, and manage them.

## Finding testimonials

Login to Senja and navigate to the Proof section: https://app.senja.io/

Your testimonials appear here automatically after you collect or import them. If you haven't added testimonials yet, you'll see a prompt to import them.

## Filter and search

Use the filters at the top to narrow your list:

- **Type:** All, Text, Video, or Case Study
- **Collections:** View testimonials you've tagged and grouped
- **Search bar:** Find testimonials using natural language

> You can't search or filter testimonials by consent. The consent info is for your own note, you can click on each testimonial to see if your customer is giving public/private consent.

## Actions you can take

Click any testimonial to access these options:

- **Update customer's details:** add photo, tagline, email, company, website, or company logo of your customer
- **Edit:** see the guide How do I edit a testimonial?
- **Trim:** Resize, trim, or convert format (see How do I edit my video testimonial?)
- **Duplicate:** copy the selected testimonial
- **Delete:** remove the selected testimonial

### Share

- **Copy link to testimonial:** get a direct link to a public testimonial page
- **Embed testimonial/video:** get an embed code to use it in your website
- **Download video:** save video testimonials (see How do I download my video testimonials?)
- **Turn into case study:** turn your testimonial into case study - also our AI automatically created a case study for you beforehand
- **Instant images from studio templates:** turn the testimonial into image
- **Create an animated video:** turn the testimonial into animated video

> Video edits can't be undone. Contact Support immediately if you make a mistake while editing.

## Troubleshooting

Can't find a testimonial? Check your filters and search bar. If it's still missing, it may not have imported correctly—try importing again.

Can't add more testimonials? Free plans limit you to 15 testimonials. Upgrade to Starter ($29/month) or Pro ($59/month) for unlimited testimonials.
