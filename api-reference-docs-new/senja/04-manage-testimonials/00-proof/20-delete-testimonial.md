# How do I delete a testimonial?

Sometimes you need to delete testimonials, for different reasons:

- Your customer asks to be removed from your testimonial page
- The testimonial is no longer relevant
- It's a mock-up or testing testimonials
- And more!

You can do it in Senja.

## How to delete testimonials in bulk

1. Go to your Proof page: https://app.senja.io/
2. Use the checkbox to choose multiple testimonials you want to delete
3. Click Delete on the popup toolbar in the bottom

## How to delete 1 testimonial

1. Go to your Proof page: https://app.senja.io/
2. Select the testimonial you want to delete
3. You'll be directed to the customer's page
4. Click Delete

The testimonial is officially deleted permanently.
