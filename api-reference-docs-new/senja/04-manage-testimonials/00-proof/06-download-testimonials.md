# How do I download my testimonials?

You can download the testimonials from Senja dashboard, either only 1 testimonial or multiple testimonials.

## Download testimonials

1. Go to your Proof page: https://app.senja.io/
2. Use the checkbox to select 1 or multiple testimonials
3. Click Export on the bottom popup toolbar

Your testimonials will be downloaded in CSV. If it has multiple questions/answers, the text testimonials will be shown in 1 cell, separated by comma. If it has multiple videos, the video testimonials will be shown in 1 cell, separated by comma.

## Download specific testimonials

1. Go to your Proof page: https://app.senja.io/
2. Set the Filter to show specific testimonials
3. Click the checkbox next to Filter button to select all (for example here it's set to have video testimonials only)
4. Click Export on the bottom popup toolbar

Your testimonials will be downloaded in CSV. If it has multiple questions/answers, the text testimonials will be shown in 1 cell, separated by comma. If it has multiple videos, the video testimonials will be shown in 1 cell, separated by comma.

## Download all testimonials

1. Go to your Proof page: https://app.senja.io/
2. Click the 3-dots next to Invite a customer
3. Click Export all to CSV

Your testimonials will be downloaded in CSV. If it has multiple questions/answers, the text testimonials will be shown in 1 cell, separated by comma. If it has multiple videos, the video testimonials will be shown in 1 cell, separated by comma.
