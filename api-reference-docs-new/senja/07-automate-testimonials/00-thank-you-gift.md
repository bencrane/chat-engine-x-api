# How Do I Send a Thank You Gift?

Thank you gifts are also known as testimonial rewards.

You can use an automation to send your customers a thank you gift when they leave you a testimonial.

## Why send a gift?

Here's some reasons you might send a customer a gift:

- Because you offered a gift to incentivize the customer to leave a testimonial
- To "close the loop" after inviting a customer to leave a testimonial
- To generate goodwill and word-of-mouth by delighting your customer

## What can I send?

An example: Trigger an email with a $10 gift code from Tremendous each time you receive a testimonial.

## What should trigger the gift being sent?

We recommend sending a thank you gift when a testimonial is approved rather than received. This ensures you can review the testimonial before the gift is sent.

## Automate thank you gifts

1. Click on Automate
2. Scroll to Send thank yous
3. Click on Connect
4. Amend the Zap to reflect your preferences

Automations are powered by Zapier and part of Senja's Starter plan.
