# Does Senja have an app?

Senja does not currently have a mobile app.

However, you can access the Senja website from your mobile phone.

We’re working hard to improve the Senja experience on mobile.

If you are looking to collect testimonials ‘in the wild’ you can use your QR code to send people to your Senja form.

You can also use Senja’s testimonial Chrome Extension to collect and share your testimonials on any website.
