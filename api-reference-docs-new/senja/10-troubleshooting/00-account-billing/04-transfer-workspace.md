# How to transfer a workspace or project to another Senja account

Need to move a workspace or project to a different Senja account?

This guide explains how to transfer individual workspaces or projects to another account, including free accounts.

Workspace transfer vs. account transferThere are two types of transfers available in Senja:Account transfer: Transfers ownership of the entire account and all its workspaces to another personWorkspace transfer: Moves a specific workspace or project to a different Senja account while keeping other workspaces in the original accountThis article covers workspace transfers.

If you need to transfer your entire account, see How to transfer ownership of your Senja account.

What you need for a workspace transferTo transfer a workspace or project to another Senja account, you'll need:Email address of the current workspace ownerEmail address of the destination account (where the workspace will be moved)Name of the specific workspace or project to transferHow to transfer a workspaceWorkspace transfers must be handled by our support team for security and billing reasons.

To request a workspace transfer:Contact Senja supportSpecify that you want to transfer a workspace (not the entire account)Provide the current workspace owner's email addressProvide the destination account's email addressInclude the name of the workspace or project you want to transferWait for our team to process the transferOur support team will verify the request and complete the workspace transfer for you.

Transfer to free accountsYou can transfer workspaces to both paid and free Senja accounts.

When transferring to a free account:The workspace will be subject to free plan limitationsPremium features may become unavailable if the destination account doesn't have a paid planThe workspace will be limited to 15 testimonials on the free planWhat happens after the transferOnce the workspace transfer is complete:The destination account owner will have full control of the transferred workspaceThe workspace will no longer appear in the original accountAll testimonials, forms, and settings within the workspace remain intactBilling for the workspace will be handled by the destination accountTeam members will need to be re-invited to the workspace in its new locationImportant considerationsBilling impact:The original account will no longer be charged for the transferred workspaceThe destination account becomes responsible for any applicable chargesIf transferring from a paid plan to a free account, some features may become unavailableAccess changes:Team members with access to the workspace will lose access after transferThe destination account owner will need to re-invite team membersExisting sharing links and embeds will continue to workQuestions about workspace transfers?

Contact our support team for assistance.
