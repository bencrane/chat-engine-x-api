# Why can't I log in to my Senja account?

If you cannot log in to your Senja account, it might be due to network issues.

Why can't I log in?

Sometimes, you may experience difficulty logging into your Senja account.

This often relates to your computer's network connection.

Our authentication provider might face connectivity issues from your end.

What causes this issue?A common cause is a Virtual Private Network (VPN). VPNs can interfere with the connection to our authentication service.

General internet connection problems can also prevent successful login.

One user reported this issue while using Speedify.

The problem later resolved itself.

How can I resolve it?

First, check your internet connection stability.

If you are using a VPN, try temporarily disabling it.

Then, attempt to log in again.
