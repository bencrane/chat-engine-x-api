# Why did I receive an email with the wrong name?

If you received an email with an incorrect name, there may be a mix-up with the name on your account.

What causes this issueThis typically happens when:Your account name was entered incorrectly during setupThere was a data entry error when your account was createdYour name information needs to be updatedHow to resolve thisOur support team can quickly fix the name on your account for you.

To get this resolved:Contact our support teamLet them know you received an email with the wrong nameProvide the correct name you'd like to useThe support team will update your account information and ensure future emails display the correct name.
