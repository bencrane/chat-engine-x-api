# How to switch from an annual to a monthly subscription

This article explains how to switch your Senja subscription from an annual to a monthly billing cycle.

This can be useful if you need more flexibility in your payments or if you're experiencing billing issues with the annual plan.

Before you begin When you switch from an annual to a monthly plan, the change takes effect immediately.

You will be charged the monthly rate for the upcoming month, and your billing cycle will be adjusted accordingly.

Switching to a monthly subscriptionFollow these steps to change your subscription from an annual to a monthly plan:Go to the Billing page in your Senja account.

On the billing page, click on the Change plan button.

You will see a pricing table with options for "Monthly" and "Yearly" billing.

Click on the Monthly tab.

Select your current plan from the list.

The button will say "Upgrade now".A confirmation dialog will appear, showing the amount you'll be charged for the next month.

Click on Change Plan to confirm.

That's it!

Your subscription has been successfully switched to a monthly billing cycle.

Your next invoice will reflect the new monthly rate.

Alternative method: Managing your subscription via StripeIf your subscription is managed by Stripe, you may also see a Manage subscription button on the Billing page.

Clicking this button will open the Stripe billing portal, where you can manage your subscription details, including the billing cycle.

TroubleshootingIf you encounter any issues while trying to switch your plan, please don't hesitate to contact our support team for assistance.
