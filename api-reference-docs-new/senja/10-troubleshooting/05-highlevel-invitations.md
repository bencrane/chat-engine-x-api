# HighLevel contact invitations are no longer supported

Direct invitations to HighLevel contacts are no longer available in Senja.

What happenedWe have discontinued support for inviting HighLevel contacts directly through our platform.

This feature is no longer available.

Alternative solutionYou can still invite your HighLevel contacts to leave testimonials using our CSV import feature:Export your HighLevel contacts to a CSV fileUse Senja's CSV import feature to send testimonial invitesThis workaround allows you to continue collecting testimonials from your HighLevel contacts without interruption.
