# Troubleshooting Video Playback: The 'Black Box' Issue

Troubleshooting Video Playback: The 'Black Box' IssueUsers have reported experiencing a 'black box' issue that prevents video previews from displaying correctly.

This article will guide you through the necessary steps to diagnose and resolve this problem.

SymptomsThe primary symptom of this issue is the appearance of a black box instead of the expected video preview.

This can occur in various situations, such as:When uploading a new videoWhile trying to play a video within the platformIn the video preview thumbnailCommon CausesThis issue can be caused by a variety of factors, including:Temporary bugs in the systemBrowser-related issuesProblems with the video file itselfNetwork connectivity problemsTroubleshooting StepsFollow these steps to resolve the 'black box' issue:Refresh the page: A simple refresh can often resolve temporary glitches.

Clear your browser cache: Cached data can sometimes interfere with video playback.

Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then restart the browser.

Try a different browser: To rule out browser-specific issues, try accessing the platform using a different web browser.

Check your video file: Ensure that your video file is not corrupted and is in a supported format.

You can try re-uploading the video or using a different video file to see if the issue persists.

Check for software updates: Ensure that your browser and any relevant plugins are up to date.

If you have tried all the steps above and are still experiencing the issue, please contact our support team for further assistance.
