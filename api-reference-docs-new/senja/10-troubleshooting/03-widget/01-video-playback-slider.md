# Video playback issues in reviews after using slider controls

This article helps you understand and resolve video playback problems that occur after manually adjusting slider controls in reviews.

What was the issue?

Previously, there was a bug that prevented videos from playing properly in reviews after you manually moved the slider control.

This affected the normal video playback experience and could interrupt your review workflow.

Current statusThe bug has been fixed.

You should now be able to play videos in reviews normally, even after manually sliding the slider control.
