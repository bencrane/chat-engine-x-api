# How Do I Edit My Video Testimonial?

## How to resize a video testimonial

If your video appears too long, it may be because it was recorded in portrait mode on a mobile device. You can fix this by re-uploading the video in landscape format.

1. Download the video from Senja.
2. Use a video editor to convert the video from portrait to landscape.
3. Re-upload the corrected video to Senja.
