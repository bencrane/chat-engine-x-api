# How Do I Turn My Testimonials into Images?

Senja helps you create beautiful testimonial images to share on your landing pages and sales pages, embed in your emails and attach anywhere you like.

Here's how to turn your testimonials into images.

To get started:

## 1. Navigate to the Studio tab

This tab is located on the sidebar here: https://app.senja.io/

## 2. Click on Images

Select the Images tab under studio navigation.

## 3. Choose from a range of customizable templates

You can always change your design from inside the editor.

## 4. Customize your design

You can choose background colors, branding options and more from the Customize side bar.

## 5. Export your image

When you're ready, click on Share in the top right corner to export your image. You can export images in a variety of formats, including PNG and JPEG.

## 6. Add to your website

It's time to add these images to your website and watch conversion rates soar.

> Pro tip: If you plan on using the same image again in the future, you can save it as a Template by clicking "Save as template" in the Share menu. This will save you time recreating the same design.
