# How do I turn my video testimonials into video embeds?

In Senja Studio, you can turn your video testimonials into video embeds.

## Steps

1. Go to your Studio page: https://app.senja.io/studio
2. Click Video embeds tab
3. Choose Single Video or Multiclip Video
4. Add the Name and choose Where do you plan to embed this widget?
5. Click Create widget
6. Customize the design as you wish:
   - **Select Testimonials:** choose the videos you want to show
   - **Templates:** change the style of the social videos
   - **Design:** change the background color/style
7. Click Save changes when you're done
8. Click Share and choose the sharing method you want
