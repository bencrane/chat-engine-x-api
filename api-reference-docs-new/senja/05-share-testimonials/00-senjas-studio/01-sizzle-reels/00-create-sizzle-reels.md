# How to create sizzle reels

Senja's sizzle reels creator uses our AI Director to automatically transform your video testimonials into compelling, ready-to-share sizzle reels. The Magic Video creator is located at the /sizzle-reel URL in your Senja account.Start creatingGo to your Studio page: https://app.senja.io/studioClick Sizzle Reel tabClick Get started Workflow overviewYou will go through 4-steps process:Duration: Choose the length of your videoTestimonials: Select the videos you want to includeDirection: Tell our AI Director the story you want to tellReview: Customize and download your finished sizzle reelStep 1: Choose the durationFirst, decide how long you want your final sizzle reel to be. This helps the AI Director pace the video and select the best clips to fit the timeframe.On the Create a magic video ✨ screen, you'll see several duration options.Click on your desired length (e.g., 30 seconds, 45 seconds, 1 minute).Click the Continue button to proceed  On this section, you can also see the remaining credit of your sizzle reels. You'll have 5 new credits every month.Step 2: Select your testimonialsNext, choose the source material for your video. You can choose up to 5 videos. If you want to change the testimonials after you select them, you can click Change Selection.  Important: Sizzle reels only works with video testimonials—it does not animate still images with audio. If you select testimonials that contain only text or still images with audio, they will not appear in your sizzle reels.Step 3: Direct the AIThis is where you give our AI Director creative instructions. You can choose a pre-defined "feel" for the video and even provide a custom focus.Choose a FeelSelect a theme that best represents the tone and style you're aiming for.Before and after: Creates a narrative focused on the transformation your customers experienced.Pains: Highlights the problems and challenges customers faced before using your product.Outcomes: Focuses on the positive results, benefits, and successes customers achieved after using your product.Highly emotional: Creates a video that pulls at the heartstrings by focusing on the emotional impact of your product.Focus (Optional)For even more customization, you can give the AI Director a specific text prompt. Use the Tell the AI director what to focus on text box to guide the narrative.Example prompt: "Create an energetic video focusing on how our product saves users time and makes their workflow easier."Once you've made your selections, click Continue. Step 4: Review, customize, and downloadNow, sit back and relax. If this is your first time generating a video, you will see the Sizzle Reels Generator start up. Our AI is hard at work:Uploading testimonialsFetching transcriptsReading timestampsFeeding to our most powerful modelGenerating scriptFilming clipsStitching everything togetherAdding royalty-free musicDownloading final result  Your sizzle reel is ready! On the final screen, you can preview your video and use the powerful editor on the left to fine-tune every detail before you download.Already have a sizzle reel?The sizzle reels that you've created will go to your Saved tab in the Studio: https://app.senja.io/studioClick on the saved sizzle reels to edit/download it You'll be directed to the Review pageMake the changes as you wish, Save changes, and Download videoEditing optionsFormatQuickly resize your video for any platform.Select from presets like Instagram reel, TikTok video, YouTube short, or Facebook adAlternatively, you can enter a custom Width and HeightSubtitlesMake your video accessible and engaging with customizable subtitles.Enable/disable the subtitlesChoose a Subtitle FontSet a Highlight Color for keywordsAdjust the Font Size and Vertical Position (Top, Middle, Bottom)MusicAdd a backing track to enhance the mood of your video.Enable/disable musicBrowse and select a track from our royalty-free library.BrandingEnsure the video matches your brand identity perfectly.Set your Primary ColorToggle the visibility of the Customer Tagline, Customer Company, and the Show Senja branding optionsOnce you are satisfied with all your customizations, click the purple Download video button to save the file to your computer. You're now ready to share it with the world!Managing your sizzle reels creditsSizzle Reels Maker operates on a credit system. You have two types of credits:Monthly Allowance: These are free 5 credits that are included with your plan and reset every monthPurchased Credits: You can buy these credits at any time and they do not expireYou can see your credit balance in the banner at the bottom on the Duration section. It will show both your purchased credits and your remaining monthly credits. The banner will display a message like:{X} credits purchased. Click here to buy more.{Y} monthly credits left. Resets on {Date}.How to buy more creditsIf you run out of credits, you can easily purchase more:In the credits banner, click on the buy more link.You will be redirected to our secure payment page, powered by StripeAfter a successful purchase, you'll be taken back to the Sizzle Reels MakerA confirmation message will appear: Credits purchased! Thanks for buying credits! It might take a few minutes for them to be show up in your account.TroubleshootingHere are some common issues you might encounter:"Video Generation Failed" ErrorIf you see a Video Generation Failed error, it could be for a few reasons.The most common is that you don't have enough credits. The error message will specify if that's the case. If so, you'll need to purchase more credits to proceed.If the error is not credit-related, try clicking the Retry button. If the issue persists, please contact our Support team."No credits left" messageIf you see a No credits left. Buy more to generate more videos. message, it means you've used up both your monthly allowance and any purchased credits.To continue creating videos, you'll need to purchase more credits using the buy more link in the credits banner.Videos cutting off mid-sentenceOur AI Director does its best to create natural-sounding edits, but sometimes it might cut a sentence a little short.If this happens, you can try regenerating the video with a slightly different Focus in the Direction step. Providing more specific instructions can often lead to a better result.No video output or still-image with audioIf your sizzle reel shows only a still image with audio or doesn't produce video clips as expected, the selected testimonials likely do not contain video content.Sizzle reel only works with video testimonials that have recorded video footage.Verify that the testimonials you selected are actual video recordings, not just images or text testimonialsIf you've uploaded a still image with an audio file, sizzle reels cannot animate it—you'll need actual video testimonialsGo back to Step 2 and ensure you're selecting testimonials that have the video iconSelected testimonials not appearing in sizzle reelsIf some of your selected testimonials don't appear in the final video:Check that all selected testimonials are video testimonials with actual video footageText-only or image-based testimonials will be filtered out automaticallyOnly testimonials with video content and transcripts can be used by the AI Director
