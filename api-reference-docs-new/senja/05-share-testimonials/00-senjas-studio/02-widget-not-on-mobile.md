# Why is my widget not displaying on mobile?

Your widget might not be displaying on mobile devices due to a specific setting in Senja's Studio.

## Why your widget isn't showing on mobile

If your widget does not appear on mobile, a setting in Studio is likely enabled. This setting prevents the widget from displaying on smaller screens.

## How to enable your widget on mobile

To make your widget visible on mobile, you need to disable this setting. Navigate to Senja's Studio and turn off the relevant option. This action will ensure your widget displays correctly on mobile devices.
