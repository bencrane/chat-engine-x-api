# Understanding Testimonial Widget Display Behaviors

This article explains the different display behaviors of testimonial widgets and helps you choose the right one.Testimonial Marquee WidgetThe Testimonial Marquee Widget is designed to slide testimonials infinitely. It continuously displays your selected testimonials in a horizontal loop. This widget creates a dynamic and engaging visual effect.Displaying Static TestimonialsIf you prefer a static display of your video testimonials, other widgets are available. These widgets present your testimonials without continuous movement. You can choose a widget that best suits your website's design.Testimonial Masonry WidgetTestimonial Lindoy ListTestimonial Carousel
