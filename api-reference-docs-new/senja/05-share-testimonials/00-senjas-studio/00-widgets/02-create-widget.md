# How do I create widget?

Turn your testimonials into embeddable widgets using the Senja Studio. Choose from 20+ widget styles and customize them to match your brand.

## What is testimonial widget?

Testimonial widget is an asset you create in Senja studio to:

- Grab more than 1 testimonial into 1 collection
- Turn your testimonials to visual attraction, adaptable to your brand identity
- Share your testimonials as link, embedded page, or image

Once you've picked a widget, you can curate the testimonials it shows, style it, and share it everywhere.

## Create a widget

1. Go to your Studio page: https://app.senja.io/studio
2. Create a widget by choosing the template that you want (you can change this later)
3. Name your widget and then click Create widget (you can change this later; you can also select the embed location to help you organize your widget)
4. Click Select Testimonials to choose how to add testimonials:
   - **Auto-add** means that all testimonials matching the criteria set will be added automatically
   - **Manual selection** means that you can cherry-pick the testimonials
5. Click Continue
6. You can set the Filters if you want a specific type of testimonials
7. Finish the selection:
   - Auto-add: click Save Filters
   - Manual selection: put the check mark on the testimonials that you want, and then click Save Selection

## Customize your widget

If you change your mind on the style of widget you want to use:

1. Open the widget
2. Choose the new style from the Widgets tab
3. Click Save changes

You can also update other elements (colors, fonts...) like so:

1. Open the widget page, click Design
2. Adjust colors, fonts, spacing, and layout until you're happy with the result
3. Click Save changes when done

> Design options vary by widget style. Explore the settings to match your brand.

## Share the widget

1. Open the widget
2. Click Share
3. You have options on how to share it:
   - Embed to your website
   - Copy the link and share to your customers
   - Download as image

> Use the free Senja Chrome Extension to quickly copy widget codes and form URLs without opening the dashboard.
