# How Can I Use My Wall of Love?

## What is a wall of love?

A Wall of Love is a curated page to showcase your testimonials.

## How to use your wall of love

You can use a Wall of Love in several ways:

- Embed it on your website
- Link to it in an email
- Use it in sales DMs
- Add it to your website navigation
- Redirect to it when someone books a call

## How to create a wall of love

You can create a Wall of Love in the Studio.
