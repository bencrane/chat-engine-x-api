# How Do I Change the Font on My Wall of Love or Widget?

We make it easy to change the font on your Wall of Love or widget.

Here's how:

1. Navigate to the widget you want to update
2. Click on the Design tab on the left-hand menu
3. Scroll down to Typography
4. Select the font you want to use in the drop down list
5. The font in your widget will automatically update
