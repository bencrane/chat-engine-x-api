# How to Add Facebook Videos to Your Wall of Love

This article explains how to add Facebook videos to your Wall of Love.

## Why you cannot directly embed Facebook videos

You cannot directly embed Facebook videos into your Wall of Love. This is due to technical limitations.

## How to add Facebook videos to your Wall of Love

You can use a workaround to include Facebook videos. First, import your videos from Facebook into your Senja account. After importing, you can then add these videos to your Wall of Love.

## Suggest a feature

Would you like to see direct Facebook video embedding in the future? You can suggest this feature on our feedback portal. Visit https://feedback.senja.io to submit your idea.
