# How Do I Link to My Wall of Love?

You can link to your Wall of Love.

To get the link to your Wall of Love, click Share.

Add it to your website menu, outbound emails, or share it in your bios.

Your Wall of Love is not embeddable. Instead you can select the mosaic widget to embed, which is the same as a Wall of Love.
