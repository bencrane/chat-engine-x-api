# How Do I Embed My Wall of Love?

If you want to add your latest social proof to your website, course or any other page, you can embed your Wall of Love. Here's how:

1. Click on Studio
2. Click on the Wall of Love you want to embed
3. Click on Share
4. Click on the copy icon

This will copy the embed code for your Wall of Love. Add the embed code wherever you want your Wall of Love to show.
