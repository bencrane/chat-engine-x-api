# How Do I Reorder Testimonials on My Wall of Love?

You can easily reorder testimonials displayed on your Wall of Love within the Studio dashboard.

## Reorder testimonials on your Wall of Love

To change the display order of your testimonials:

1. Go to your Studio dashboard.
2. Select the Wall of Love you want to edit.
3. Find the section containing your testimonials.
4. Drag and drop the testimonials into your preferred sequence.
5. Save your changes to update the Wall of Love.
