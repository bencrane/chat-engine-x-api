# How Do I Add a Custom URL to My Wall of Love?

You can add a custom URL to your Wall of Love.

1. First navigate to your Wall of Love and click on Share
2. Click on the pencil edit icon
3. Here you can either edit the URL slug (my-wall-of-love), or set up a custom domain
