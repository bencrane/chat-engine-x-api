# How Do I Share a Link to a Testimonial?

1. Click on the testimonial you want to share
2. Click on Copy link to testimonial - The testimonial viewer will open, scroll down until you see Copy link to testimonial. Click on it
3. Your testimonial link is copied and ready to share
