# Can I Send My Senja Testimonials Elsewhere [Google Reviews]?

## How to encourage reviews on other platforms

While Senja doesn't have a direct feature for this, you can add links to other review platforms. Place links to sites like Trustpilot or Capterra on your form's Welcome page or Thank you page.

Use markdown format to add the links. This gives customers a simple way to leave a review on another site.

We also suggest importing your testimonials from other platforms to Senja. This helps you manage all your reviews in one place.
