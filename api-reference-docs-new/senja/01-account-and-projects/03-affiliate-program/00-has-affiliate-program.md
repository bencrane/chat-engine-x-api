# Does Senja have an affiliate program?

Yes, Senja has an affiliate program that helps you earn by sharing Senja with your audience.

Program perksSenja's affiliate program offers:30% recurring commission for life on all revenue from customers you referPersonalized referral link via FirstPromoter dashboardReal-time tracking of your referrals and payoutsResources, assets, and monthly opportunities to help you succeedLearn more and join at senja.io/affiliates.

How the program worksWhen someone clicks your referral link and upgrades to a paid plan (Starter at $29/month or Pro at $59/month), you earn 30% of their subscription revenue for as long as they remain a customer.

Track your referrals and earnings through your affiliate dashboard.

How to applyTo apply for the affiliate program, you'll need to provide information about your audience and promotion plans:Your biggest or most engaged audience channel (e.g., website, blog, SEO)What your audience wants to sell more of (e.g., SaaS subscriptions)Your URL (e.g., mapleads.io)Your audience size (e.g., 8,500)How you currently use Senja personally (e.g., Free account)Details about your first Senja promotion and your plan to promote itProgram termsBefore applying, review these program terms:You cannot refer yourselfYou cannot run paid ads promoting SenjaYou cannot add Senja to a discount or AI directoryYou can present Senja as a sponsor in your contentYou must intend to promote Senja in the next 20 days Questions about the affiliate program?

Contact us at partners@senja.ioWhen you're ready, apply to join the Senja affiliate program.
