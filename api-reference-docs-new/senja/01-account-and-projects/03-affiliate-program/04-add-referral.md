# How to add referral as Senja affiliate

As a Senja affiliate, referrals are tracked automatically when people use your unique affiliate link.

You don't need to manually add them.

Find your affiliate linkYour affiliate link is shared when you are approved as affiliate and contacted by our team.

Locate the email to see your affiliate link.

Another way is:Login to FirstPromoter: https://senja.v2.firstpromoter.com/homeSee your affiliate link on the Promoter pageHow referrals are trackedWhen someone clicks your affiliate link (format: https://senja.io?via=xxxxx) and upgrades to a paid plan, the referral is automatically tracked in your FirstPromoter dashboardYou'll earn 30% recurring commission for as long as they remain a customerYou can view all your referrals, clicks, and earnings in real-time at senja.firstpromoter.com.

Add your affiliate link to Powered By badgesIf you use Senja for your own projects, you can embed your affiliate link in the Powered By Senja badge that appears on your forms, widgets, and Walls of Love.

Go to your Project Settings > Powered By: https://app.senja.io/settings/powered-byAdd your link in Affiliate link for Powered By sectionClick Save settings Your affiliate link will now be embedded in all Powered By Senja badges across your projects.

You'll need a paid Senja plan (Starter or Pro) to add your affiliate link to Powered By badges.

Adding pre-approval referralsIf you referred someone before becoming an approved affiliate, contact partners@senja.io with proof of the referral (email, chat, or text message).

The team can add it retroactively after reviewing your submission.

Share your affiliate link on social media, in newsletters, or in direct conversations to maximize referrals.

The more places it appears, the more you'll earn.
