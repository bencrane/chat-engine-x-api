# Can I add a custom domain?

Yes.

You can use your own subdomain (like subdomain.yourdomain.com) for your Senja forms, Walls of Love, and individual testimonial links.

Custom domains are available on paid plans only.

See pricing here.

1.

Upgrade to a paid planIf you're on the free plan, upgrade first.2.

Go to Domain settingsNavigate to Project Settings > Domain: https://app.senja.io/settings/domain
3.

Enter your subdomainType the subdomain you want to use (like testimonials.yourdomain.com) and click Save. 
4.

Add DNS recordsSenja will generate DNS records for you.

Copy these details and add them to your domain provider's DNS settings. DNS changes can take up to 24 hours to propagate, but usually complete within an hour.

Once your DNS records are verified, your custom domain will be active.
