# How to manage a project I created for a client

If you created a Senja project for a client, you have three options to manage the situation effectively.

Option 1: Rename your projectThis option works best if you only have a few testimonials in the project.

Export any existing testimonials from the projectDelete the testimonials from your accountRename your project to use it for your own businessYou can change your project's name, slug and URL in your project settings.

For help with exporting testimonials, see: How do I download (export) my testimonials?

Option 2: Change your email addressThis approach transfers your entire account to your client.

Change the email address on your account to your client's emailCreate a new Senja account using your original email addressFor step-by-step instructions, see: How do I change my email address?

Option 3: Create a new account for your clientThis option gives your client full ownership of the project.

Ask your client to create their own Senja accountAlternatively, create an account for them using their email addressContact Senja support to transfer project ownership to the new accountThis method ensures your client has complete control over their testimonials and project settings.
