# How to transfer ownership of my Senja account

Transferring account ownership allows you to hand over full administrative control of your Senja account to another person.

How to transfer ownershipAccount ownership transfers must be handled by our support team for security reasons.

To request a transfer:Contact Senja supportProvide these  info:The email address of the current account owner (admin)The email address of the person who will become the new account ownerWait for our team to process the transferOur support team will verify the request and complete the ownership transfer for you.

What happens after the transferOnce the transfer is complete:The new admin will have full control of the accountThe previous admin will lose administrative accessAll account settings, projects, and data remain intactTransferring individual workspacesIf you only need to transfer a specific workspace or project (not the entire account), you can request a project transfer instead.

This allows you to:Move individual projects to different Senja accountsTransfer projects to free accountsKeep other projects in your current accountLearn more about projects transfers in this detailed guide.
