# How do I delete my account?

This action is irreversible.

Your subscription, projects, testimonials, widgets, Walls of Love and other account related data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted.

Go to your Account settings (click the arrow next to your avatar) Click Delete Account button Your account and all its data will be deleted in a few secondsIf you see an error message or if you can't delete your account, you will need to contact Senja Support team.
