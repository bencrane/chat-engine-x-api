# Is Senja available in my language?

The Senja platform is currently only available in English.

However, you can collect and display testimonials in over 20 other languages.

Most translation features are exclusively available on the Pro plan.

Translating testimonialsYou can translate your testimonials into over 20 languages to reach a global audience.

Learn more in sharing testimonials in specific languages.

Translating formsYou can customize your forms to be in any language by editing the text directly.

Senja doesn't currently support automatic form translations or multiple languages within a single form—you'll need to create separate forms for each language.

Learn more in localizing your form language.

Request a new languageWant to see a specific language added?

Share your suggestion on the feedback board.
