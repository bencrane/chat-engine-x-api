# How do I remove Senja branding?

You need a paid accountCustomers of Senja on a Starter or Pro plan can remove Senja branding from their widgets, forms, images, videos and Walls of Love.

If you are on the Free plan click here to upgrade.

Note:When you upgrade to a Starter of Pro plan, all new widgets you create will be created without Senja brandingSenja branding on your Wall of Love is automatically removedSenja branding on your forms is automatically removedSenja branding on your videos is automatically removed Senja branding is also known as Made with Senja or Powered By.

How to remove Senja brandingRemove branding in generalGo to your Project Settings > Powered By: https://app.senja.io/settings/powered-byToggle off the Show Senja Powered By sectionClick on Save settings Senja branding will be removed from all assetsRemove branding from FormsGo to your Forms page: https://app.senja.io/formsEdit the form that you wantGo to Form tabClick DesignPut the toggle off in Show Senja Powered By Remove branding from widgets or Walls of LoveGo to your Studio page: https://app.senja.io/studioPick the widget/Wall of Love you want to remove the Senja branding fromGo to DesignScroll down until you find Remove Senja branding sectionPut the toggle off in Show Senja Powered By That’s it, your widget no longer has Senja branding 💜
