# Can I get an invoice for my payments?

You can view and download your past invoices from your Senja dashboard.

How to get the invoicesGo to your Billing pageClick View/Download Invoice  If your old invoices are not there, contact Senja Support team and mention the payment dates of the invoices that you want.
