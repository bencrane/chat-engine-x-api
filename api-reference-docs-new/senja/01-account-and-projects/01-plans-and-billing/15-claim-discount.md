# How do I claim my discount?

Apply discount codes during Stripe checkout to get promotional pricing on your Senja subscription.

This covers nonprofit discounts (30% off for life) and promotional codes.

Available discounts: Nonprofit/NGO/Charity (30% off for life - learn more) and promotional codes from partners/campaigns.

Apply your code BEFORE completing payment on the Stripe checkout page.
