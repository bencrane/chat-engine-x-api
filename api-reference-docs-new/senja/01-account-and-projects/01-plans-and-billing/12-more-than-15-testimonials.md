# What happens after I collect more than 15 testimonials?

The Free plan includes 15 testimonials (text and video combined).

This is a total limit that doesn't reset monthly.

This limit only applies to Free plan users.

Starter ($29/month) and Pro ($59/month) plans have no testimonial limits.
