# How do I add my company name and billing address to an invoice?

When upgrading to a paid Senja plan, you can add your company name and billing address directly on the checkout page.

This information will appear on your invoices.

This applies when upgrading from the free tier to Starter ($29/month) or Pro ($59/month) plans.

How to add your billing detailsWhen you're ready to upgrade:Click Upgrade your plan to get started from your Billing page: https://app.senja.io/billing On the checkout page, manually enter your billing address in the provided fieldsTo add your company name, tick the I am purchasing as a business checkbox at the bottom of the payment formEnter your business name in the field that appears Your company name and billing address will automatically appear on all future invoices once saved.
