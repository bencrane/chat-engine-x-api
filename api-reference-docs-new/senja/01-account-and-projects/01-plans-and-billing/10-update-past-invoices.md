# Can I update past invoices with new billing information?

Changes to your billing information only apply to future invoices.

Past invoices remain unchanged and cannot be edited directly.

You can update your billing details anytime at app.senja.io/billing.

New invoices will automatically reflect the updated information.

Why past invoices don't updateOnce an invoice is issued, it becomes a permanent financial record.

This ensures accurate accounting and tax compliance for both you and Senja.

Getting a corrected invoice for a past paymentIf you need an invoice with updated billing details for a previous payment:Refund the original payment — Contact support to process a refund for the invoice you need correctedUpdate your billing information — Go to your billing settings and enter the correct detailsMake a new payment — This generates a fresh invoice with your updated information Refunding and reprocessing a payment may cause a brief interruption to your service.

Your account will remain active once the new payment is processed.

For minor corrections like typos in your company name or address, contact support — we may be able to help without requiring a refund.
