# What to do if my signup/upgrade payment failed?

Payment failures during signup or upgrade are usually declined by your bank for reasons like insufficient funds, fraud protection, or an invalid CVV. A notification explaining the decline appears during checkout.

Steps to resolve payment issuesRe-enter your card details and try againTry a different cardContact your bank to check for blocks or restrictionsIf your card details are correct and your bank confirms no blocks, contact our Support team for help Payment failures happen more frequently in certain countries due to stricter banking regulations
