# How to collect testimonials on your platform

Collecting testimonials on your platform helps build trust and credibility with potential customers.

## Main collection methods

You can collect testimonials using several proven approaches:

### Embed testimonial forms

Add Senja forms directly to your platform or website. This allows customers to submit testimonials without leaving your site.

### Send invitation emails

Invite customers to share their experience through personalized email invitations to your testimonial form.

### Automate collection

Set up automated workflows to request testimonials at optimal moments in your customer journey.

### Share form links

Distribute your testimonial form link through various channels like social media, newsletters, or direct messages.

## Platform-specific implementation

While these methods work across all platforms, the specific implementation varies depending on your setup. You can adapt these general approaches to work with:

- Course platforms
- E-commerce sites
- Service-based businesses
- SaaS applications
- Any custom platform

## Next steps

Choose the collection method that best fits your workflow and customer interaction patterns. Start with one approach and expand to multiple methods as you build momentum.
