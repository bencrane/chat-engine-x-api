# How to approve testimonials automatically

By default, testimonials you collect need manual approval before you can use them in widgets or share them. You can enable auto-approval to automatically approve all testimonials you collected. Auto-approval is available for all account types.

## Enable auto-approval for a form

1. Go to Forms in your dashboard: https://app.senja.io/forms
2. Click the Edit button on the form you want to configure
3. Navigate to the Settings tab
4. Put the toggle on next to Approve testimonials
5. Click Save changes when done

4-5 stars testimonials will be automatically approved in the Proof page: https://app.senja.io/

Your settings are saved automatically. All future testimonials collected through this form will be approved automatically.

## Enable auto-approval in auto-import

1. Go to Import > Auto-import: https://app.senja.io/import/auto
2. Click on the 3-dots on your connected platform
3. Click Settings
4. Set the Minimum rating to the star level that you want to approve automatically

Read How to auto-import testimonials from other platforms to see how you connect your platform for this automated import.
