# TikTok Ads

Send your event data from RudderStack to TikTok Ads.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[TikTok for Business](<https://www.tiktok.com/business/en>) is a global platform designed to give brands and marketers the solutions to be creative storytellers and meaningfully engage with the TikTok community.

With solutions that can deliver seamlessly across every marketing touchpoint, TikTok for Business offers brands an opportunity for rich storytelling through a portfolio of full-screen video formats that appear natively within the user experience.
