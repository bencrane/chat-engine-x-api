# Gainsight PX

Send your event data from RudderStack to Gainsight PX.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Gainsight PX](<https://www.gainsight.com/product-experience/>) is a popular product experience platform that offers cutting-edge product analytics and product engagement features. It allows product teams to optimize their customer adoption and onboarding flows and design relevant product experiences through email, dialogue, and effective user guides.
