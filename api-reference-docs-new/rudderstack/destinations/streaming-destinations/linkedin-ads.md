# LinkedIn Ads Destination Beta

Send your event data from RudderStack to LinkedIn Ads.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[LinkedIn Ads](<https://business.linkedin.com/marketing-solutions/cx/22/07/ads-for-linkedin>) is an online advertising platform that helps you drive business growth. You can send your marketing data directly to LinkedIn by leveraging their [Conversions API](<https://www.linkedin.com/help/lms/answer/a1680223>) to measure and optimize the performance of your LinkedIn campaigns.
