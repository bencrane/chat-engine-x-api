# comScore

Send your event data from RudderStack to comScore.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[comScore](<https://direct.comscore.com/>) is a self-service portal that helps you tag all your digital assets and ensure the right exposure of your online content like web pages, videos, mobile apps, ad networks, and more.
