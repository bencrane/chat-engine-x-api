# Facebook Custom Audience

Send your event data from RudderStack to Facebook Custom Audience.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Facebook Custom Audience](<https://developers.facebook.com/docs/marketing-api/audiences/guides/custom-audiences>) is a popular targeting tool that lets you find people on Facebook who are interested in your business. You can also create custom audiences through customer lists, Facebook engagement, and website/app traffic.
