# Bloomreach Catalog Beta

Send your catalog data from RudderStack to Bloomreach.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Bloomreach](<https://www.bloomreach.com/en>) is a digital experience platform. It helps businesses personalize online experiences through AI-driven content recommendations, search optimization, and marketing automation to enhance customer engagement and drive sales.

RudderStack supports syncing catalog data from your [Reverse ETL source](<https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/sources/reverse-etl/>) to Bloomreach.
