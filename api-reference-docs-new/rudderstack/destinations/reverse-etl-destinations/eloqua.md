# Eloqua Beta

Send your event data from RudderStack to Eloqua.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Eloqua](<https://www.oracle.com/in/cx/marketing/automation/>) is Oracle’s marketing automation platform that helps B2B marketers and organizations manage their marketing campaigns and sales lead generation.

RudderStack supports sending data to Eloqua from your [Reverse ETL source](<https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/sources/reverse-etl/>).
