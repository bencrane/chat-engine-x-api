# Import Number from Twilio

## Overview

This guide explains how to integrate a Twilio phone number—whether new or existing—into your Vapi account as you expand your agent infrastructure.

## Prerequisites

You'll need access to a Twilio account to proceed.

## Steps to Import

**Step 1: Obtain a Twilio Phone Number**

If you don't already have one, purchase a number through your Twilio console's phone number purchase section.

**Step 2: Retrieve Your Credentials**

Navigate to the API keys and tokens section of your Twilio account to locate your Account SID and authentication token.

**Step 3: Import via Vapi Dashboard**

1. Open the Phone Numbers section in your Vapi dashboard and select the import option.
2. Input your phone number and Twilio credentials, then proceed with the import.

**Step 4: Begin Using Your Number**

Once imported, the number is ready for use with your assistants for both incoming and outgoing calls. You can configure additional settings through the phone number detail page.

---

**Key Takeaway:** The process involves securing a Twilio number, extracting authentication details, importing through Vapi's interface, and then activating it for agent communications.
