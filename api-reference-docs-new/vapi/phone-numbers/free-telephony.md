# Creating Free Phone Numbers

## Overview
The Vapi platform allows users to create complimentary phone numbers for use with assistants or squads.

## Step-by-Step Process

1. **Navigate to Phone Numbers**: Access the "Phone Numbers" tab within your Vapi dashboard.

2. **Initiate Creation**: Select the "Create a Phone Number" option.

3. **Select Area Code**: In the "Free Vapi Number" section, enter your preferred area code. Currently, "only US phone numbers can be directly created through Vapi."

4. **Receive Number**: The system automatically assigns a random phone number at no cost. Note that "it takes a couple of minutes for the phone number to be fully activated" during which incoming calls won't work.

## Key Limitations & Details

**Geographic Restrictions**: Free numbers are limited to the United States at this time.

**Account Limits**: Each wallet supports up to 10 complimentary numbers, a constraint designed to maintain service reliability.

**International Considerations**: "You can still bring in global numbers using our phone number import feature," though these incur charges.

**Cost Structure**: There are no hidden fees—the free service reflects operational efficiencies and partnership advantages.
