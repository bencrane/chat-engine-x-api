# Plans & Pricing

## Pricing Model
BlitzAPI operates on a flat-rate subscription with unlimited usage. The offering eliminates per-request fees and overage charges, enabling users to budget predictably regardless of volume.

## Subscription Tiers

### Unlimited Leads ($399/month)
Includes:
- Waterfall ICP search
- Company search
- Company enrichment
- Employee finder
- Domain-to-LinkedIn mapping

Provides access to:
- 380M+ contacts
- 60M+ companies
- 5 requests per second

### Unlimited Email ($499/month) — Most Popular
Builds on Leads tier by adding:
- Email enrichment
- 62M+ verified emails
- 97% email accuracy

Targets cold email teams and outbound agencies.

### Unlimited Phone Numbers ($599/month)
Extends Email tier with:
- Phone enrichment
- 40M+ mobile numbers
- US only coverage

Designed for multi-channel outreach and call centers.

## Rate Limits

All tiers operate at identical rate limits:
- 5 requests per second
- 5 concurrent requests
- Recommended 10-20 second timeouts

## Value Proposition
The flat-rate model encourages iteration without cost penalties. Users can enrich 100 leads or 100,000 at identical pricing.

## Free Trial
All new accounts receive **1,000 free trial credits** before commitment.
