# Early Access Program

Collaborate with RudderStack’s Product team on building new features.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


The **Early Access Program** is run by RudderStack’s [Product team](<mailto:product@rudderstack.com?subject=I%20have%20a%20question%20about%20the%20Early%20Access%20Program>) and serves as a collaborative way get feedback from users on new features and experimental projects.

## Alpha and Beta features

We release Early Access features in two different stages: [Alpha](<https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/get-started/alpha-and-beta-features/alpha-features/>) and [Beta](<https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/get-started/alpha-and-beta-features/beta-features/>).

#### Alpha features

[Alpha features](<https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/get-started/alpha-and-beta-features/alpha-features/>) are very early in development and are often experimental. They are functional and we encourage you to try them, but we highly recommend [connecting with the Product team](<mailto:product@rudderstack.com>) before using them in production.

> ![info](/docs/images/info.svg)
> 
> Not every Alpha feature will be released. Depending on feedback, certain experimental projects may be deprecated.

#### Beta features

[Beta features](<https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/get-started/alpha-and-beta-features/beta-features/>) are stable and have been tested by our team and early users, but are actively being changed and improved based on feedback before the MVP is finalized and made generally available.

## Join the Early Access Program

To join the Early Access Program, [reach out](<mailto:product@rudderstack.com?subject=I%20have%20a%20question%20about%20the%20Early%20Access%20Program>) to the Product team.

If you are only interested in specific features, keep an eye on the [Release Notes](<https://rudderstack.com/docs/releases/>), and reach out when you see an alpha or beta feature you’d like to try.
