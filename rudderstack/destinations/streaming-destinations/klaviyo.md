# Klaviyo Destination

Send your event data from RudderStack to Klaviyo.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Klaviyo](<https://www.klaviyo.com/>) is a powerful ecommerce platform that lets you boost your business revenue. It offers unique features like category-based segmentation and various event triggers based on page views, purchases, email engagement, etc.
