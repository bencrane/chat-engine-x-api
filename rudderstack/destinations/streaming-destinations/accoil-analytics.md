# Accoil Analytics Destination Beta

Send your event data from RudderStack to Accoil Analytics.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Accoil](<https://www.accoil.com>) is a a product analytics platform built specifically for B2B go-to-market (GTM) teams like product, sales, marketing, customer success, and support. It allows you to track feature adoption, monitor user journeys, and enhance activation and conversion rates.

> ![info](/docs/images/info.svg)
> 
> This integration is built by the Accoil team. [Contact the Accoil support team](<https://help.accoil.com>) for any assistance.
