# June

Send your event data from RudderStack to June.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[June](<https://www.june.so/>) is a lightweight product analytics tool. It helps you define and measure product usage intuitively by tracking user retention, active users, acquisition funnel, engagement, feature usage, and more.
