# Vero

Send your event data from RudderStack to Vero.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Vero](<https://www.getvero.com/>) is a popular marketing automation tool which lets marketing and product teams seamlessly communicate with their customers via personalized multichannel messaging.

RudderStack supports Vero as a destination to which you can seamlessly send your customer data.
