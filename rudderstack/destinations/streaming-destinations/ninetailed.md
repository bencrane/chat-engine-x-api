# Ninetailed Destination Beta

Send your event data from RudderStack to Ninetailed.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

[Ninetailed](<https://ninetailed.io/>) is an experimentation and personalization platform. It helps you increase user conversions and drive business growth through personalized customer experiences.
