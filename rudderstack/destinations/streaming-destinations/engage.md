# Engage

Send your event data from RudderStack to Engage.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Engage](<https://engage.so/>) is a marketing automation tool. It enables you to deliver personalized marketing campaigns and automate onboarding flows to drive conversions and customer retention.

RudderStack supports Engage as a destination to which you can seamlessly send your customer data.
