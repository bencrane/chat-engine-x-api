# Google Analytics 4 Destination

Send your event data from RudderStack to Google Analytics 4.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Google Analytics 4](<https://analytics.google.com/>) is an analytics service that enables you to measure traffic and engagement across your websites and apps. It can be used for websites, apps, or both together - whereas Universal Analytics properties only support websites.
