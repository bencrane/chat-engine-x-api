# Pinterest Tag

Send your event data from RudderStack to Pinterest Tag.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Pinterest Ads Tag](<https://ads.pinterest.com/>) allows Pinterest to track your visitors as well as their actions. It gives you a detailed conversion report related to all customer activities like product signups, purchases, and more.
