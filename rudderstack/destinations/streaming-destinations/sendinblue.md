# Brevo (previously Sendinblue)

Send data from RudderStack to Brevo.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

[Brevo](<https://www.brevo.com/>) (previously Sendinblue) is a cloud-based digital marketing and sales platform. It automates your marketing tasks and lets you manage your email campaigns, leads, transactional emails, and personalized conversations with customers.
