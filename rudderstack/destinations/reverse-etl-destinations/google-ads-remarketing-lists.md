# Google Ads Remarketing Lists (Customer Match)

Send your event data from RudderStack to Google Ads Remarketing Lists.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[Google Ads Remarketing Lists](<https://support.google.com/google-ads/answer/6276125?hl=en>) lets you send a list of users (audience list) to Google Ads and run targeted advertising campaigns without having to update the user list manually.

RudderStack supports Google Ads Remarketing Lists as a destination where you can send your audience list.
