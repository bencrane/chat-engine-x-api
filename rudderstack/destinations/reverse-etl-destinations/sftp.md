# SFTP (SSH File Transfer Protocol) Beta

Transfer your warehouse data to downstream apps via RudderStack by leveraging SFTP.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


[SFTP](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/SSH_File_Transfer_Protocol>) stands for SSH File Transfer Protocol. It is a secure file transfer mechanism that allows you to access, transfer, and manage your files over a reliable data stream.

RudderStack lets you transfer your warehouse data at scale to your downstream apps by leveraging SFTP.
