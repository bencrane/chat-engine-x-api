# TikTok Audiences

Send your event data from RudderStack to TikTok Audiences.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


TikTok provides the [Audiences](<https://ads.tiktok.com/help/article/audiences?lang=en>) feature to identify and target the right audience to improve your ad performance.

You can use the **TikTok Audiences** destination to sync the right audiences from your warehouse to TikTok.
