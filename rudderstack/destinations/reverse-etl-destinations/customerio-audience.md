# Customer.io Audience Beta

Send your event data from RudderStack to Customer.io Audience.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


RudderStack’s **Customer.io Audience** integration leverages the [Customer.io Segments](<https://docs.customer.io/journeys/segments/#what-are-segments>) feature which lets you group users sharing similar characteristics as segments and use them as recipient lists, filters, campaign triggers, conversions, and more.
