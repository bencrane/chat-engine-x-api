# Webhook Destination

Send events from RudderStack to a custom webhook destination.

* * *

  * __less than a minute

  * 


Webhooks let you send the events tracked by the RudderStack [SDKs](<https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/sources/event-streams/sdks/>), [cloud apps](<https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/sources/event-streams/cloud-apps/>), and [Reverse ETL](<https://www.rudderstack.com/docs/sources/reverse-etl/>) sources. They are useful in cases where you want to apply some custom logic on the event payload before sending it to your preferred destinations.
