# TCPA Consent Guide

## Overview
This guide explains consent requirements under the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) for outbound calls via Vapi's voice agent service. The TCPA does not apply to inbound calls.

## Consent Types by Call Category

**Marketing calls** require Prior Express Written Consent (PEWC), which mandates:
- A signed written agreement from the recipient
- Clear disclosure that the consumer authorizes "calls using artificial or prerecorded voice" and that consent isn't required for purchases

**Non-marketing calls** need Prior Express Consent (PEC)—a less stringent standard requiring "express and obvious consent." The FCC permits PEC for any number related to an existing transaction.

## Key Requirements

- **Affirmative opt-in is mandatory.** Pre-checked consent boxes are insufficient.
- **Revocation rights** must be honored. Consumers can withdraw consent "at any time through any reasonable means."
- **Marketing** broadly encompasses calls promoting purchases, rentals, investments, or advertising commercial availability.

## Important Limitation

This content does not constitute legal advice and omits coverage of other telemarketing regulations like federal Do-Not-Call rules. Consult a telemarketing compliance attorney for additional guidance.
