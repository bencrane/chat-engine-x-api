# Vapi Voices Documentation

## Overview

Vapi Voices represents "our carefully curated selection of high-quality voices designed to simplify your voice AI implementation." The platform offers a focused collection rather than overwhelming users with dozens of options.

## Key Benefits

The selection prioritizes three main advantages: simplicity by eliminating extensive testing, quality through vetted natural-sounding voices, and pre-optimization for immediate deployment.

## Available Voices

The platform currently features 11 active voices across various demographics:

**Male voices** include Elliot (Canadian, 20s), Rohan (Indian American, 20s), Nico (American, 20s), Kai (American, 30s), Sagar (Indian American, 20s), Godfrey (American, 20s), and Neil (Indian American, 20s).

**Female voices** include Savannah (Southern American, 20s), Emma (Asian American, 20s), and Clara (American, 30s).

The newer additions—Emma, Clara, Nico, Kai, Sagar, Godfrey, and Neil—are marked as recent releases.

## Implementation

Users can integrate voices by selecting from the active collection and incorporating them into assistants or squads. The documentation notes that "legacy voices were retired recently" and references a migration guide for affected users.

## Use Cases

Typical applications include customer service automation, content narration, and interactive conversational systems.
