# AssemblyAI Transcriber Integration

## Overview

AssemblyAI provides "Universal-Streaming, a purpose-built speech-to-text model delivering ultra-fast transcripts in ~300ms" with intelligent endpointing and superior accuracy for voice agents.

## Key Features

The service addresses common transcription challenges like misheard account numbers, awkward pauses, and premature cutoffs, enabling more natural voice interactions.

## Setup Instructions

To integrate AssemblyAI with your assistant:

1. Navigate to the Assistants tab in your Vapi dashboard
2. Select your assistant and access the Transcriber tab
3. Choose "assembly-ai" from the Provider dropdown menu

## Language Support

Currently, the Universal-Streaming model supports English exclusively.
