# Fast Track Guide

Even though the USPS loves working with us, they still have to charge for sending mail. Even if you are on the Developer edition of Lob, before you get started we'll ask you to input your payment information, then add [Lob Credits](https://help.lob.com/account-management/billing/lob-credits), so you are prepared to send mail!

## Step 1: How do you plan to execute your mail?

<details>

<summary>In the Lob dashboard</summary>

* Send mail via [Campaigns](https://help.lob.com/print-and-mail/reaching-your-audience/campaign-audience-guide), our user-friendly interface built atop our APIs. We support one-time (1:many, targeted outreach), or automated (1:1, based on a trigger) campaigns.&#x20;

</details>

<details>

<summary>Programmatically</summary>

* [API Documentation](https://docs.lob.com/) - Every developer knows that the path to getting a successful integration up and running starts here. You’ll be provided an example of each type of Lob API request and response, so you can build an integration that works for you/your team.
* [Campaigns API](https://help.lob.com/print-and-mail/building-a-mail-strategy/campaign-or-triggered-sends/send-campaigns-via-the-api) - What if you want to send large-scale direct mail campaigns but don’t want to execute them manually via the campaign tool? Well, you’re in luck! Learn how you can achieve the same efficiency but via the Campaigns API so you can automate your sends.

</details>

## Step 2: Who are you sending to?

<details>

<summary>Audience </summary>

* For a one-time campaign, you'll need your audience data (.csv); see here for how to [format your addresses](https://help.lob.com/print-and-mail/reaching-your-audience/all-about-addresses).
* To set up and send automated or triggered campaigns, you'll want to connect directly to you customer data. Learn more about our Integrations here.

</details>

## Step 4: How do you plan to measure the success of your mail?

<details>

<summary>Lob dashboard</summary>

* [Tracking](https://help.lob.com/print-and-mail/getting-data-and-results/tracking-your-mail) - One of the most powerful advantages of using Lob is that you can have visibility of any individual mail piece as it moves across the entire print production and delivery process.
* [Mail Analytics](https://help.lob.com/print-and-mail/getting-data-and-results/mail-analytics) - Every team measures unique insights to ensure you hit your targets. Discover a few views that cover some of the most important metrics you should care about.
* [Metadata](https://help.lob.com/print-and-mail/building-a-mail-strategy/managing-mail-settings/using-metadata) - Gain a deeper understanding of your mail data at a mail piece, recipient, batch, or campaign level by adding your own data labels.

</details>

* [Metadata](https://help.lob.com/print-and-mail/building-a-mail-strategy/managing-mail-settings/using-metadata) - Gain a deeper understanding of your mail data at a mail piece, recipient, batch, or campaign level by adding your own data labels.
