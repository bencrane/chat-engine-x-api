SIP
Session Initiation Protocol
Session Initiation Protocol (__SIP__) is a signaling protocol for initiating, terminating and modifying user sessions over an IP network.
__How Twilio Uses SIP__
__Using SIP to connect to Twilio is one way__ to get access to Twilio's global reach and powerful automation and scripting functions.
You can connect your on-site or virtual Private Branch Exchange (PBX) with Twilio's servers. Here's our overview of __connecting your existing SIP communications__ infrastructure to us.
