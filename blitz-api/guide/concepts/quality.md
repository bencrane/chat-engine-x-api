# Quality Standards

## Core Philosophy
BlitzAPI positions itself as a "Verification Engine" rather than a traditional data provider. The key principle: **"Data is a liability unless it is verified."** They prioritize precision over volume, rejecting the industry practice of selling outdated contacts.

## Zero-Guesswork Approach
The platform avoids pattern-matching email generation (like `firstname.lastname@company.com`). Instead, they employ deterministic verification, only delivering contacts when multiple signals confirm:
- Current active role status
- Email deliverability today

Their standard: **"We would rather return 'Not Found' than deliver a risky lead."**

## Data Maintenance
BlitzAPI implements a 30-day hygiene cycle to counter B2B data decay (3-5% monthly). Features include:
- Re-validation of verified emails at minimum monthly intervals
- Deep SMTP handshake validation for catch-all domains
- Automatic removal of failed contacts from circulation

## Three-Layer Verification Model

### Layer 1: Professional Signal
Confirms real identity via professional network footprints.

### Layer 2: Cross-Source Matching
Associates contact information across compliant data sources.

### Layer 3: Deliverability Proof
Validates current email validity and safe delivery.

## Coverage Metrics

| Data Type | Coverage | Accuracy |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Verified Emails | Global | 97% (no pattern guessing) |
| Phone Numbers | US only | ~90-95% mobile accuracy |
| Contacts | Global | LinkedIn-based |
| Companies | Global | Full LinkedIn dataset |

## Automation Benefits
High accuracy enables confident pipeline automation through CRM/email tool integration while maintaining sender reputation through low bounce rates.
