MarkdownClosing the dispute for a charge indicates that you do not have any evidence to submit and are essentially dismissing the dispute, acknowledging it as lost.
The status of the dispute will change from needs_response to lost. *Closing a dispute is irreversible*.
### ParametersNo parameters.
### ReturnsReturns the dispute object.
POST /v1/disputes/:id/close```
