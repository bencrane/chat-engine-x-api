# How do I change my project name?

You can rename your project at any time from the project settings.

The project name is visible to you in your dashboard but won't affect your public-facing testimonial widgets or forms.

How to change your project nameClick the settings icon (gear icon) at the bottom of your left sidebar Make sure you're on the General tabUpdate the text in the Project Name fieldClick Save settings  Changing your project name only updates how it appears in your dashboard.

Your project slug and URL remain the same unless you also change them from this Project Settings page.
