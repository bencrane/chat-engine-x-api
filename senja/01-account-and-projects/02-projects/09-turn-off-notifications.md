# How do I turn off notification emails?

Senja sends email notifications when you receive new testimonials.

You can turn these off in your settings.

Account typeAvailable on all plans (Free, Starter, Pro)Turn off notification emailsGo to Project Settings > Notifications: https://app.senja.io/settings/notificationsSet the notifications that you wantTick for turning it onUntick for turning it offClick Save settings
