# How do I cancel my paid plan?

Cancelling your paid plan means downgrading to Free plan.

Senja will not delete your account or your project.

Steps to cancel paid planGo to your Billing page: https://app.senja.io/billingClick Or cancel your subscription Follow the on-screen instruction to finalize cancellationSubscription Cancellation ConfirmationAfter you cancel your subscription, you will receive an email confirming the cancellation.

Your account will automatically downgrade to the Free plan at the end of your current billing period, and you will not be charged again.

You've successfully canceled your subscription.

Check your email for a confirmation message.

What happens when I downgrade to Free plan?

Downgrading your plan will result in the following changes to your account:You will lose access to paid features at the end of your billing cycleAfter your paid plan expires, you will have access to paid features for a grace period of 10 daysAll your existing assets will stay: forms, testimonials, widgets, and Walls of LoveYou will not be able to add new formsAll your Widgets and Walls of Love will have the Senja brandingIf you have less than 15 testimonials, you can still collect until the max cap of 15 testimonials is filledIf you have more than 15 testimonials already, new testimonials will be locked/hiddenCancellation failedIf you encounter an issue when trying to cancel your paid plan, contact Senja Support team.
