# What are the differences between the Free and Paid plans?

Senja offers a generous Free plan with up to 15 testimonials plus two paid plans—Starter ($29/month) and Pro ($59/month)—with unlimited testimonials and advanced features.

Here's what sets each plan apart.

We don't offer a traditional free trial for paid plans.

Our Free plan is very generous and lets you explore many features.

Start with the Free plan and upgrade whenever you're ready for more advanced features and higher limits.
