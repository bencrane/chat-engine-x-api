# Can I request a refund?

If you'd like to request a refund for your subscription, we handle these requests on a case-by-case basis.

Reasonable requestsCommon situations where refunds are granted include:Immediate cancellation after upgradingSwitching from monthly to yearly billingAccount not actively usedBusiness closure or financial hardshipHow to request a refundTo request a refund, contact our Support team with your account details and reason for the request.

Our team will review and respond within 1-2 business days.
