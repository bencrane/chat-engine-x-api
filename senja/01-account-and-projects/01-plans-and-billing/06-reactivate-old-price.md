# Can I reactivate my subscription at the old price?

This article explains our policy regarding subscription reactivation and pricing.

Reactivating your subscriptionWhen you reactivate a subscription, it will be at the current pricing.

We do not support old pricing for new or reactivated subscriptions.

Resubscribing after a payment failureIf your subscription was canceled due to a payment failure, you may be able to resubscribe at your old price.

If you were on our Starter plan, you can use the promo code STARTERLEGACY at checkout to keep your old pricing.

If you have already resubscribed at the new price, please contact our Support team, and we will issue a refund and apply a coupon to your subscription so you will be billed at your old price moving forward.

Need a refund instead?

If you accidentally upgraded to the wrong plan or billing cycle, you may be eligible for a refund.

See our guide on how to get a refund for accidental subscription upgrades for more information.
