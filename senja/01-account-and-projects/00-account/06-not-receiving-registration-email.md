# What to do if I'm not receiving registration email

If you are not receiving your registration email, here are some steps you can take to resolve the issue.

Check your email foldersFirst, check your email inbox and spam folders.

Search for keywords like Senja or Sign-in link.

Sometimes, emails can be misfiltered.

Try a different email addressIf the issue continues, try using another email address.

For example, use a Gmail or Outlook account.

This helps determine if your email provider is blocking the message.

Contact supportIf you still do not receive the email, please contact us.

We can resend the registration link for you.
