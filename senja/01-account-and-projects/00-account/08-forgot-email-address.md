# I forget what email address I use for Senja

If you've forgotten which email address you used to sign up for Senja, don't worry.

You can find your registered email address by locating your Senja widget ID and contacting our support team.

Find your Senja widget IDYour Senja widget ID is a unique identifier for your testimonial widget.

You can find it in the HTML code of the website where you've embedded the widget.

Go to the webpage where your Senja widget is embeddedRight-click on the widget and select Inspect or Inspect Element from the context menuThis will open your browser's developer toolsIn the developer tools, look for a <div> element with the class "senja-embed".

The widget ID is the value of the data-id attributeHere's an example of what the code looks like:  <div data-id="33eb83f5-b4f6-429c-b427-bbb94fd86f18" data-mode="shadow" data-lazyload="false" class="senja-embed"></div>    In the example above, the widget ID is 33eb83f5-b4f6-429c-b427-bbb94fd86f
18.

Contact supportOnce you have your widget ID, please contact our Support team.

Please include the widget ID in your message, and we'll be able to find the email address associated with your account.

For security reasons, we can only provide the email address to the owner of the account.

We may ask for additional information to verify your identity.
