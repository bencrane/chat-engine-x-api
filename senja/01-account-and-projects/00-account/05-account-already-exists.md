# What to do if my account already exists when signing in?

If you see an account already exists message when trying to sign in, this typically means you previously created an account using a different sign-in method (e.g., Google, email link, or password).

Try different sign-in methodsBefore contacting support, try signing in using:Google – if you originally signed up with GoogleEmail magic link – check your inbox for a sign-in linkPassword – if you set one during signupStill can't access your account?

Contact Senja Support team with the email address you used to create your account.

The team will investigate and help you regain access.
