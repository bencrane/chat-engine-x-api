# How to find my registered email and website

Your Senja account has two key identifiers: the email address you use to log in, and the website URL connected to your project.

Here's how to find both.

Find your registered email addressThe fastest way is to click your user avatar in the sidebar.

Your email appears at the top under Signed in as.

Alternatively:Go to Account settings (click the arrow next to your avatar) Under Personal information, find the Email field Your account email is used for logging in and notifications.

It's separate from your project website.

Find your connected websiteGo to Project Settings in the bottom side of the sidebar Under Project URL, you'll see the website connected to your project  If you have multiple projects, switch between them by clicking the project name in the bottom-left corner.

Each project has its own connected website.
