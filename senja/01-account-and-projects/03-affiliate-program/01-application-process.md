# Senja affiliate application process: status, approval times, and next steps

After submitting your Senja affiliate application, our marketing team manually reviews each submission to ensure quality partnerships.

This guide covers approval times, how you'll be notified, and what to do if your application is rejected.

Applications are typically reviewed within 1-4 weeks.

You'll receive email notification at the address you provided.

What happens after you applyYour application enters our review queue where our marketing team evaluates each submission.

We look for affiliates who align with Senja's mission of helping creators and businesses leverage social proof.

Response times vary based on:Application volumeCompleteness of your applicationTeam availabilityHow you'll be notifiedYou'll receive an email notification at the address you provided:Application approved: Welcome email with instructions to access your affiliate dashboardApplication rejected: Details about why your application wasn't approved and guidance for reapplying Check your spam folder if you don't see our email within 4 weeks.

Checking your application statusThere isn't a self-service portal to check your status.

If you've been waiting longer than 4 weeks, contact partners@senja.io for an update.

Once approved, log into your affiliate dashboard through FirstPromoter to track your progress and access your affiliate link.

Common reasons for rejectionApplications are typically rejected when we don't have enough information to evaluate your audience and promotional strategy:Insufficient audience information: No details about your website, social media following, or target audienceMissing promotional strategy: Unclear how you plan to promote SenjaIncomplete application: Missing required fields or minimal responsesPoor audience fit: Your audience doesn't align with Senja's target market of creators and businessesHow to reapply after rejectionYou can reapply with improved information.

To increase your chances of approval:Provide detailed audience insights: Include website URLs, social media profiles, subscriber counts, and audience demographicsExplain your promotional strategy: Describe how you plan to share Senja with your audienceShow your content quality: Link to examples of your best content or previous product recommendationsDemonstrate Senja knowledge: Show you understand what Senja does and why it would benefit your audienceWait at least a few days before reapplying to give yourself time to improve your application substantially.

Once you're approvedAfter approval, you'll receive a welcome email with next steps.

You can then:Access your affiliate link and dashboardDownload promotional materialsStart earning 30% commission on all referralsTrack your performance and payoutsWhat to do while waitingWhile your application is under review:Try Senja yourself: Sign up for a free account to understand the product betterPlan your content: Prepare blog posts, social media content, or email campaignsStudy successful affiliates: Check out examples on our affiliate pageBuild your audience: Focus on growing your platform and engagement Questions about your application?

Contact partners@senja.io for assistance.
