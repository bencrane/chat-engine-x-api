# How to auto-import testimonials from other platforms

Senja can automatically import your new testimonials from supported platforms. We call this functionality Auto-import. Auto-import is available on Senja's paid plans.

## How to do it

1. Go to your Import > Auto-import page: https://app.senja.io/import/auto
2. Click Connect source
3. Choose the platform to connect
4. Click Continue

You can connect multiple platforms in Auto-import, just repeat the process for each platform.

## More setup

Once connected, you will have the list on your Auto-import page. Make sure that all platforms are enabled (the toggle on).

You can do more setup by clicking the 3-dots:

- Click **Settings** to see the additional configuration:
  - Connection name
  - Minimum rating, to limit the imported testimonials from specific rating (1 star to 5 stars)
  - Auto-tag imported testimonials, to add tags automatically when the import is happening
  - Auto-translate imported testimonials
  - Auto-approve imported testimonials
- Click **Import now** to run the import without waiting for the auto-sync
- Click **Disconnect** if you want to remove that platform from your Auto-import page

## What's next?

New testimonials coming to the connected platforms will be automatically added to your Proof page when the auto-sync runs. The auto-sync happens every 12 hours, at 12 AM and 12 PM GMT.

If you encounter an issue, contact our Support team to help you.
