# Import testimonials from Homestars

Senja lets you import reviews from your Homestars company profile and keep them updated with auto-sync. Before you begin: You'll need the URL for your Homestars company profile.

## What you can do

- Import all reviews from your Homestars profile at once
- Select individual reviews to import specific testimonials
- Auto-sync to automatically pull in new reviews (paid plans only)

Auto-sync is available on paid plans. The free plan is limited to 15 testimonials total.

## How to import from Homestars

1. Go to Import > Import from web: https://app.senja.io/import/web
2. Click the Homestars icon
3. Paste your link under Homestars company URL. Use a Homestars profile URL like `https://homestars.com/profile/your-company`
4. Click Import testimonials
5. Choose which testimonials you want to import, or select all to import everything

Your Homestars reviews are now in Senja and ready to share.
