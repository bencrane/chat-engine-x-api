# Import testimonials from Airbnb

Senja lets you import guest reviews from your Airbnb listings and automatically sync new ones as they come in. This feature is available on all plans. Auto-sync requires a paid plan.

## What you can do

- Import all reviews from a specific listing at once
- Select individual reviews to import specific testimonials
- Auto-sync to automatically pull in new reviews (paid plans only)

## How to import from Airbnb

1. Login to your Airbnb and open the listing you want to import the reviews from
2. Click Share
3. Click Copy Link
4. Go to Import > Import from web: https://app.senja.io/import/web
5. Click the Airbnb icon
6. Paste your link under Airbnb listing URL
7. Remove the query parameters from your URL. Your copied link will look like: `https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/12345678?source_impression_id=...` Delete everything after the room ID (starting with the ?), so it looks like: `https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/12345678`
8. Click Import testimonials
9. Choose individual testimonials or click Select all testimonials to import everything
10. Click Import

Your Airbnb reviews are now in Senja and ready to share.
