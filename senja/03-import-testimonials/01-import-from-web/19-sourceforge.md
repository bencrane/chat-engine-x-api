# Import testimonials from SourceForge

Senja lets you import testimonials from your SourceForge page and keep them in one place. This feature is available on all plans. Auto-sync availability depends on your plan.

## What you can do

- Import all testimonials from your SourceForge page at once
- Select individual testimonials to import specific entries
- Auto-sync new testimonials if available for SourceForge (paid plans only)

## How to import from SourceForge

1. Go to Import > Import from web: https://app.senja.io/import/web
2. Click the SourceForge icon
3. Paste your link in SourceForge project URL field
4. Click Import testimonials
5. Choose which testimonials you want to import, or select all to import everything

Your SourceForge testimonials are now in Senja and ready to share on your website.
