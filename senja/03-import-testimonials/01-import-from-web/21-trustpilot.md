# Import testimonials from Trustpilot

Use the Trustpilot import to bring your reviews into Senja so you can share them anywhere. Before you begin: You'll need your Trustpilot company URL and a Senja account.

**Account type:** Available on Free, Starter, and Pro plans.

## What you can do

- Import all reviews from your Trustpilot page at once
- Select individual reviews to import specific testimonials
- Auto-sync to pull in new reviews automatically

## Step by step

1. Go to Import > Import from web: https://app.senja.io/import/web
2. Click the Trustpilot icon
3. Paste your link under Trustpilot page URL
4. Click Import testimonials
5. Choose which testimonials you want to import, or select all to import everything

Your Trustpilot reviews are now in Senja and ready to share.

## Important note about Trustpilot policies

Trustpilot may send warnings if you display their reviews on your website without using their paid widgets. Some users remove Trustpilot logos using custom CSS to address this. We're working on a permanent solution with our legal team. If you receive a warning from Trustpilot, contact Support for guidance.
