# How can I automatically tag and filter Google Reviews by campus?

This article explains how to automatically tag and filter Google Reviews by campus.

## Connect your Google organization

Connect your Google organization to Senja. This connection is unique for each Google Place ID. It allows you to manage reviews from different locations.

## Enable auto-tagging

Enable auto-tagging after connecting your organization. This feature automatically assigns tags to your Google Reviews. These tags are based on the specific campus or location.

## Filter testimonials by campus

You can filter testimonials using these tags. Apply filters in your Widgets or on your Wall of Love. This lets you showcase reviews for each campus separately.
