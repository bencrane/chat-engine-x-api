# Import testimonials from Whop

Senja lets you import testimonials from your Whop page and keep them in one place. This feature is available on all plans. Auto-sync availability depends on your plan.

## What you can do

- Import all testimonials from your Whop page at once
- Select individual testimonials to import specific entries
- Auto-sync new testimonials if available for Whop (paid plans only)

## How to import from Whop

1. Go to Import > Import from web: https://app.senja.io/import/web
2. Click the Whop icon
3. Paste your link in Whop product URL field
4. Click Import testimonials
5. Choose which testimonials you want to import, or select all to import everything

Your Whop testimonials are now in Senja and ready to share on your website.
