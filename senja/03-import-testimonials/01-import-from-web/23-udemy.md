# Import testimonials from Udemy

Senja lets you import testimonials from your Udemy page and keep them in one place. This feature is available on all plans. Auto-sync availability depends on your plan.

## What you can do

- Import all testimonials from your Udemy page at once
- Select individual testimonials to import specific entries
- Auto-sync new testimonials if available for Udemy (paid plans only)

## How to import from Udemy

1. Go to Import > Import from web: https://app.senja.io/import/web
2. Click the Udemy icon
3. Paste your link in Udemy course URL field
4. Click Import testimonials
5. Choose which testimonials you want to import, or select all to import everything

Your Udemy testimonials are now in Senja and ready to share on your website.
