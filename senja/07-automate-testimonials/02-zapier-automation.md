# Testimonial Automation with Zapier

Testimonial automation via Zapier is available on Senja's paid plans.

You can use our Zapier integration to automate your testimonial collection, management, sharing and notifications.

## Auto-collect automations

You can automatically invite customers to leave you a testimonial at the perfect moment. The testimonial invites will show in Senja, and we'll handle the reminders too.

Once a customer submits a testimonial, we automatically stop any scheduled follow-up reminders for them.

You can enable automation in testimonial collection using Forms, 1 form at a time.

1. Go to your Forms page: https://app.senja.io/forms
2. Edit the form you want to automate
3. Go to Trigger > Automatic
4. Choose the platform and click Connect
5. You will be directed to your Zapier dashboard
6. Follow the on-screen steps
7. On the final part, regardless the form you choose to edit, you have the option to choose different form in the same Project
8. Move to different Project to automate the form you have there

More information about auto-collect in forms is available in the Auto-collect testimonials article.

## Sharing automations

You can automate your testimonial management and sharing with Senja. Build your own recipe with Zapier, or use our pre-made templates.

1. Go to Automate page: https://app.senja.io/automations
2. On the Zapier tab, choose the platform to Connect
3. You will be directed to your Zapier dashboard
