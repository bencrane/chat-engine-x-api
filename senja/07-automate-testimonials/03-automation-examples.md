# Examples of Testimonial Automations

Senja's Zapier integration lets you automate testimonial collection and responses. Connect Senja to 7,000+ apps to trigger actions based on testimonials or automatically request feedback.

Learn how to set up automations in Testimonial automation with Zapier.

## Popular automation ideas

- Email a discount code when someone submits a video testimonial
- Send a testimonial request 5 hours after a Calendly call
- Import testimonials from Airtable or Notion databases to Senja
- Post a notification to Slack when you receive a new testimonial
- Save video testimonial backups to Dropbox automatically
- Send handwritten thank-you notes when testimonials arrive
- Trigger testimonial requests when you tag contacts in your CRM

Pro plans include pre-built Zapier templates in the Automate tab for faster setup.
