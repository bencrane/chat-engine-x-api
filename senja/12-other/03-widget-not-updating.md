# Why isn’t my widget updating automatically?

If your widget isn’t updating properly, it usually means one of the following things.1.

You’re using a caching system/pluginIf your site is built with WordPress, more often than not you’ll be using a cacheing plugin like Litespeed or WPRocket.

These plugins prevent Senja from updating your widget automatically.

You’ll need to clear your cache using the instructions provided by your caching plugin.

To fix this ongoing, simply follow these steps:Litespeed: https://publytics.net/en/blog/how-to-exclude-items-from-being-cached-by-litespeed-cache-a-step-by-step-guideWPRocket: https://docs.wp-rocket.me/article/976-exclude-files-from-defer-jsFor other caching plugins, contact their support to find out how to exclude Senja.

For the URL to exclude, use https://widget.senja.io/*
2.

Your widget’s filters are configured incorrectlyIf your widget isn’t using your latest testimonials follow these stepsClick on Select testimonials at the top right of the widget studio.

Make sure your widget’s filters are set to approved.

Make sure the filter conditions on the left are up to date
