# Adding popup widgets to Framer

Popup widgets  let you display testimonials as subtle notifications on your Framer site.StepsFirst, create your popup widget and copy the embed code.Then add it to Framer:Click Site Settings > GeneralPaste your code at the end of the <body> tag section Questions? Contact Senja Support team.
