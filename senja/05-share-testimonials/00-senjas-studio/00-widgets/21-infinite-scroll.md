# Does the review widget have an infinite scroll feature?

This article explains the current availability of an infinite scroll feature for review widgets.Current AvailabilityCurrently, the review widget does not support an infinite scroll feature. This functionality is not yet available.Requesting the FeatureYou can submit a feature request for infinite scroll. Visit the Senja feedback portal to add your idea. Other users can upvote your suggestion, and you will be notified when this feature is implemented.
