# How to show my widget in my viewer's language

Senja lets you auto-translate your widget for your viewers based on their browser settings. Translation features require a Pro plan.Available languages: English, Spanish, Japanese, Mandarin Chinese, German, French, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, Korean, Arabic, Dutch, Polish, Swedish, Turkish, Hindi, Indonesian, Thai, Vietnamese, CzechAuto-translate for viewersTo show testimonials in each visitor's browser language:Go to your Studio page: https://app.senja.io/studioOpen your widgetIn your widget editor, click DesignToggle on Automatically show testimonials in the visitors languageChoose the default languageIf your viewer's language is not part of Senja list, the will see the defaultDefault language options: German, English, French, IndonesianClick Save changes  If you want to have more languages to be featured, share your feedback in our public board.
