# Can I export my widget as an image?

Sometimes you make an awesome widget, but embedding it into your website isn’t an option (e.g. your site doesn’t support custom code).Don’t worry! Senja makes it easy to share your widgets as images instead.Here’s how:1. In the Studio, click ShareHead to https://app.senja.io/studio and click Share 2. Click on the export as image tab 3. Choose your Image WidthYou can input your custom size in the text field below or export it in 1080 4. Head to Your Site and Add The ImageAnd you’re done!Looking for a guide to adding your testimonial images and widgets to specific platforms? Head here.You can also turn any testimonial into an image in the Studio.
