# How to reorder testimonials in your widget

How to reorder testimonials in your widgetYou can customize the order of testimonials in your widget to highlight your best reviews first.Steps to reorder testimonialsOpen your widget in your Studio page: https://app.senja.io/studioClick on DesignClick on Reorder testimonials Rearrange as you wishSort by date, rating, videos first, or randomOrder by newest/oldest firstClick the Pin icon to pin a testimonial to the top Pinning testimonialsWhen you pin a testimonial, it will appear at the top of your widget. You can pin multiple testimonials to prioritize the most important ones for your visitors to see first.
