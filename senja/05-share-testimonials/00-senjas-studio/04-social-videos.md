# How to create social videos

Social videos in Senja are short, engaging video reels created from your video testimonials for sharing on platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts. You'll need at least one video testimonial to create social videos.

## Steps

1. Go to your Studio page: https://app.senja.io/studio
2. Click Social Videos tab
3. Choose the template that you want
4. Customize the design as you wish:
   - **Select Testimonials:** choose the videos you want to show
   - **Templates:** change the style of the social videos
   - **Design:** change the background color/style
5. Click Share to export your video as MP4
