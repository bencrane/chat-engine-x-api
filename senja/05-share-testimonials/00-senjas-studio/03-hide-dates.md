# How to hide dates on your widgets or Walls of Love

You can hide the dates on your widgets, including your Walls of Love, for a cleaner look. This is a per-display setting, so you'll need to configure it for each Wall of Love and Widget individually.

## Hiding dates on a Wall of Love

1. Navigate to the Studio page
2. Click on the Wall of Love you want to edit or create a new one from the available templates
3. In the editor, go to the Design tab
4. In the Content section, toggle off the switch for Show dates and likes
5. Click Save changes

Your changes will be saved, and the dates will be hidden on your Wall of Love.

## Hiding dates on a widget

1. Navigate to the Studio page
2. Click on the widget you want to edit or create a new one
3. In the editor, go to the Design tab
4. Find the Show dates and likes option and turn it off
5. Click Save changes

Your changes will be saved, and the dates will be hidden on your Widget.

> Note: The term "proofs" has been updated to "Social Proof Studio", which includes Walls of Love and Widgets.
