# How Do I Create Wall of Love?

Turn your testimonials into embeddable Wall of Love using the Senja Studio. Choose from 5 templates and customize them to match your brand.

## Create a Wall of Love

1. Go to your Studio page: https://app.senja.io/studio
2. Choose Walls of Love tab
3. Choose the style that you want (you can also change it later)
4. Name your Wall of Love
5. Click Create wall of love
6. Click Select Testimonials to choose how to add testimonials
   - Auto-add means that all testimonials matching the criteria set will be added automatically
   - Manual selection means that you can cherry-pick the testimonials
7. Click Continue
8. You can set the Filters if you want a specific type of testimonials
9. Finish the selection
   - Auto-add: click Save Filters
   - Manual selection: put the check mark on the testimonials that you want, and then click Save Selection

## Customize your Wall of Love

If you change your mind on the style of Wall of Love you want to use:

1. Open the Wall of Love
2. Choose the new style from the Templates tab
3. Click Save changes

You can also update other elements (colors, fonts...) like so:

1. Open the Wall of Love page, click Design
2. Adjust colors, fonts, spacing, and layout until you're happy with the result
3. Click Save changes when done

Design options vary by Wall of Love style. Explore the settings to match your brand.

## Share the Wall of Love

1. Open the Wall of Love
2. Click Share
3. You have options on how to share it
   - Copy the link and share to your customers
   - Embed to your website
