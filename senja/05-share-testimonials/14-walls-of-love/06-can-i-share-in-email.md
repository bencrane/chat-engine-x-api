# Can I Share a Wall of Love in an Email?

You cannot embed a Wall of Love in an email. However you can share the link to your Wall of Love in an email (see How do I link to my Wall of Love?).

You can also add testimonial images to your emails using the Senja Chrome Extension (see Share and download testimonials with Senja's Chrome Extension).
