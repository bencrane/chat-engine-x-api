# What is Wall of Love?

Wall of Love is a shareable page that displays your testimonials and reviews. It's hosted by Senja and can be linked from your bio, emails, or navigation menu.

You can create unlimited Walls of Love, each featuring as many testimonials as you like (up to 120 per Wall). Style them with templates or customize every detail.

Wall of Love is available on all plans, including Free.

## Key features

- **Drag-and-drop reordering** - Arrange testimonials in any order
- **Interactive filters** - Let visitors filter by tags with custom emojis and names
- **Template library** - Choose from pre-built themes like Whimsical, Noire, Pastel, and Hong Kong
- **Customizable design** - Change fonts, toggle dates/likes, adjust typography
- **Video support** - Include video testimonials alongside text reviews
- **Easy sharing** - Get a shareable link or embed code for your site

To create a wall of love, read the full guide in How do I create Wall of Love?

## Wall of Love versus widgets

Wall of Love is a full-page showcase you link to. Widgets are compact embeds (like carousels or popups) you add to specific sections of your site.

Use a Wall of Love for a dedicated testimonial page. Use widgets to display social proof throughout your website.
