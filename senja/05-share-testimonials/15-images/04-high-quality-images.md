# How to Get High Quality Images of Your Testimonials

Creating professional-looking testimonial images helps you showcase customer feedback effectively across your marketing materials.

## Use image templates in Studio

To generate high-quality testimonial images, use the image templates available in Studio.

The Studio provides pre-designed templates that automatically format your testimonials into visually appealing images. These templates ensure consistent branding and professional presentation of your customer feedback.

## Steps to create testimonial images

1. Navigate to Studio in your Senja dashboard
2. Browse the available image templates
3. Select a template that matches your brand style
4. Choose the testimonial you want to convert to an image
5. Customize the design if needed
6. Export your high-quality testimonial image

The exported images are optimized for sharing across social media, websites, and marketing materials.
