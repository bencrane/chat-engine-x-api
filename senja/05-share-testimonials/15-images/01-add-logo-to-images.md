# How Do I Add My Logo to Testimonial Images?

1. From testimonial image creation in the Studio, click on Branding
2. If you don't have a logo, you can click Upload Image under logo. Alternatively, select an existing logo
3. After selecting a logo, use the options below to control the position and style of the logo on your image
