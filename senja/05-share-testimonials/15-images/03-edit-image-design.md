# How Do I Edit the Design of My Testimonial Image?

You can customize your testimonial images in the Studio to match your brand. All design changes update in real-time as you edit.

## Edit your image design

1. Go to Studio in your sidebar: https://app.senja.io/studio
2. Choose your saved image
3. Use the Customize panel on the sidebar to edit
4. Use the Resize button on top right to change the dimension

## Customization options

**Template**
- Choose from pre-built designs
- Save your custom designs to Your Templates for reuse

**Background**
- Solid colors
- Image using the provided patterns or upload your own
- Gradient

**Branding**
- Add your logo
- Adjust logo position and size

**Size**
- Set custom dimensions
- Use platform presets (social media sizes)

**Content**
- Show/hide avatar
- Show/hide rating

Save your customized design as a template to quickly create matching images for future testimonials.

## Export your image

Once you're happy with the design, click Share in the top-right corner and download as PNG/JPEG/ZIP. You can also Copy to Clipboard.

If you want to use the same image customization for your future image widgets:

1. Click Share
2. Choose Save as template
