# Share and Download Testimonials with Senja's Chrome Extension

Senja's Chrome extension lets you share your testimonials anywhere on the web. We want to make it easier for you to get more visitors and more sales thanks to social proof!

Download our free testimonial extension here.

## The powerful actions

Here are some of the powerful actions you can take with the Chrome extension:

- Access all your latest testimonials, and paste them as images or copy
- Access the links for your forms and the code for your widgets with two clicks
- Download your video testimonials and create social videos

## Sharing testimonials with Chrome extension

1. Open Senja Chrome extension from any page you'd like to share your testimonials
2. Click the link icon on the testimonial of your choice to copy the image to the clipboard
3. Click the X to hide the extension so you can paste the testimonial in your desired location
4. Paste the testimonial in your desired location (e.g., Notion, a webpage, etc.)

You can also share just the text:

1. Click on copy text icon to capture the text only
2. Click the X to move Senja Chrome extension out of your view
3. Paste the text testimonial into the page

## Sharing forms using Chrome extension

1. Open Senja Chrome extension from any page you'd like to share your form link to
2. Choose the Forms tab on the extension
3. Find the form you want
4. Click the copy URL icon to capture the form link
5. Paste the link on the page that you want

## Sharing widgets using Chrome extension

1. Open Senja Chrome extension from any page you'd like to share your form link to
2. Choose the Widgets tab on the extension
3. Find the widget you'd like to share, then click on the copy icon to get the URL, or click the embed icon to copy the embed code
4. Paste the widget embed code or widget URL on the page you want
