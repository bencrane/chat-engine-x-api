# How to collect more testimonials with Senja

You can collect more than just customer testimonials with Senja to gather a wider range of valuable content.

## Strategies to collect more testimonials

### Enable auto-collect

Automatically request testimonials from your customers. You can enable this feature here.

### Import from other sources

Bring in existing testimonials from various platforms. Import your testimonials here.

### Expand your content types

Use Senja to collect diverse content beyond customer testimonials.

You can gather case studies, feedback, and partner testimonials. Collect video job applications, affiliate shoutouts, and how-to videos.

This broadens your content library and provides more social proof.
