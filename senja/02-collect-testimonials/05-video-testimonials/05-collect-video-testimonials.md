# How to collect video testimonials with Senja

Senja allows to you to collect video testimonials using different methods.

## How to collect video testimonials

- Using Senja form, following the guide What are collection forms?
- Importing automatically from other platforms, following the guide How to auto-import testimonials from other platforms
- Importing videos from web, following the guide in Import from web collection
- Importing from CSV/XLSX, following the guide Can I import testimonials in bulk?
- Manually import video files that you have already, following this guide How can I manually import video testimonials?

## What you can do with imported videos

Once your video testimonials are uploaded, you can:

- Share them in emails
- Add them to widgets on your website
- Create remix videos
- Use them across all Senja features
