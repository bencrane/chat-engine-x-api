# Can I ask for multiple videos in a form?

When you use a testimonial collection form to request a video testimonial, you can ask your customers to record and submit multiple videos.

## How to do this

Use Senja Forms 2.0 to create multiple questions.

- You can set all questions to have video responses only
- You can also do a mix, 1 question for video, another for text
- If you set the questions to accept videos and text, your customers can choose either to response in video or text

## How does it look on my page?

All submitted testimonials will be recorded in your Proof page: https://app.senja.io/

- The responses from 1 customer will be presented in 1 entry
- If it's a mix of text and video, you can see it on the preview
- You can download the text and video following these guidelines:
  - How do I download my testimonials?
  - How do I download my video testimonials?

Senja will auto-merge multiple videos into multiclip. You can see the individual videos and multiclip in your Proof page.
