# How do I translate my testimonials?

Use bulk translation to create new language versions of your testimonials so you can share the right version anywhere.

> Before you begin: You need a Pro plan and at least one testimonial selected for translation.

**Account type:** Pro plan

## Step by step

1. Go to your Proof page
2. Click the testimonial you want to translate
3. Click Edit
4. Click Add translation and then choose the Language from the dropdown
5. Click Generate Translation
6. The translation will be loaded
7. Click Add Translation if you're happy with it
8. Now you can navigate between different languages
