# How do I send a thank you note?

It is important to thank customers for sharing a testimonial.

Senja's thank you feature allows you to:

- Record and send your customer a video thank you, and mark a testimonial as thanked
- Or, mark a testimonial as thanked when you have thanked the customer outside of Senja

## Send a thank you note

1. Go to your Proof page: https://app.senja.io/
2. Choose the customer/testimonial you want to thank
3. Click Thank customer
4. On the popup, review the message
5. If you're not happy with it, follow the guide in How do I customize the thank you email to update it
6. You can also record/upload a thank you video
7. Click Send thank you when it's done

It is now marked as thanked.

## Notes

The Thank customer function will not work if:

- You already send a thank you note (automated by the form, or manual sending)
- There is no email address in the customer's details
