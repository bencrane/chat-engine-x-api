# What do approved and unapproved mean?

When someone submits a testimonial, Senja marks it as unapproved so you can review it before sharing.

> Before you begin: You'll need access to the form settings if you want to enable auto-approval.

## Approved testimonials

- Can be seen in all accounts
- Can be seen in the Chrome Extension
- Can be used in the Studio
- Can be shared with a link

## Unapproved testimonials

- Can be seen in your account
- Cannot be used in the Studio
- Can be shared with the share link

## Where do you see the status?

1. Go to your Proof page: https://app.senja.io/
2. On the right side of each block, you can see if it's approved or unapproved
3. Click on the testimonials to read/review the testimonial

## How to approve unapproved testimonials in bulk

1. Go to your Proof page: https://app.senja.io/
2. Filter the status to show only Unapproved ones
3. Select all, using the checkbox next to Filter
4. You can also select some of them by clicking the checkbox one by one
5. Click Approve on the bottom popup toolbar

> You can also unapprove approved testimonials using the same way.

## Enable auto-approval for a form

1. Go to your Forms page: https://app.senja.io/forms
2. Edit your form
3. Go to Settings tab
4. Enable (put the toggle on) the Approve testimonials

This will automatically publish 4-5 star video reviews to your widget.

> Tip: You can also enable auto-approval during imports to publish testimonials without manual review.
