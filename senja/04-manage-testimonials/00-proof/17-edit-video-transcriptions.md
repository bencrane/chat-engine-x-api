# Can I edit video transcriptions?

Senja automatically transcribes your video testimonials. You can edit these transcriptions to correct spelling, grammar and other mistakes.

We display transcriptions on your testimonial videos. The transcriptions are also used to help you find testimonials with search.

Want to see and edit your transcriptions? Here's how:

1. Select the video testimonial in Proof
2. Click the edit icon on the screen that loads
3. Scroll down and click on Edit Transcript
4. Edit the text you'd like
5. Then click on Save transcript
