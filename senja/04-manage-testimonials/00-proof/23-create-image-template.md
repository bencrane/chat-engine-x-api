# How to create image template

Image templates let you save your customized testimonial designs for reuse. Once created, you can apply them to new testimonials instantly without redoing your styling. You'll need at least one testimonial in your Senja account to create an image template.

## Creating image templates from Proof page

1. Go to Proof page: https://app.senja.io/
2. Choose any testimonial
3. Click Share
4. Click Create an image
5. Update the Background, Branding, and Resize
6. Click Share > Save as template

## Creating image templates from Senja Studio

You can create an image template by updating the existing template in the Studio page, and save it as your own.

1. Go to Studio > Images: https://app.senja.io/studio#images
2. Choose the style that you want
3. Update the Background, Branding, and Resize
4. Click Share > Save as template

Now your template is available on Your Templates section.

## Customize your design

Use the Customize panel to adjust your template's appearance. You can modify:

- **Content** - Text, ratings, and testimonial details
- **Background** - Colors, gradients, or images
- **Branding** - Your logo and brand elements

On the Background update, you can add your own image as background:

1. Choose Images as Background Style
2. Click Upload Image

> To reuse a saved template, simply click on it from the Templates section and your design will be applied instantly.
