# How to recover a deleted testimonial?

You can recover a deleted testimonial by contacting customer support. You will need to provide the name and email of the customer who submitted it.

## What information you need

When contacting support to recover a deleted testimonial, provide:

- **Customer name and email:** The name and email address of the person who submitted the testimonial
- **Deletion timeframe:** When you deleted the testimonial (approximate date and time)
- **Email notifications:** Screenshots or copies of email notifications you received from Senja about this testimonial

## Restoring deleted forms and their testimonials

If you accidentally deleted an entire form (not just individual testimonials), both the form and all its associated testimonials can be restored. This is a common issue when:

- Clearing browser cache while editing forms
- Accidentally deleting the wrong form when managing duplicates
- Making bulk changes to multiple forms

For complete instructions on restoring deleted forms and their testimonials, see the dedicated guide: How to restore a deleted form and its testimonials.

## How to contact support

To recover your deleted testimonials or forms, contact the support team. The team typically responds within a few hours and can often restore your content the same day.

Our support team can usually restore deleted testimonials and forms within hours of your request. Make sure to include all the required information above to speed up the process.
