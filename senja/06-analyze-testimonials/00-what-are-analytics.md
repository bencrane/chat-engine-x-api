# What Are Testimonial Analytics?

Senja tracks analytics for your forms, widgets, and Walls of Love so you can measure engagement and conversion.

## Form analytics

Find your form analytics at Forms:

- **Views:** Unique visits since March 2024
- **Response rate:** Form conversion since March 2024

Form analytics were launched in March 2024, so data is only available from that date forward.

## Widget and Wall of Love analytics

Find analytics for widgets and Walls of Love in the Studio.

All widget and Wall of Love metrics show data from the last 30 days only.

### Widgets

- **Views:** Number of times your widget was viewed
- **Engagements:** Number of times visitors clicked, swiped, or played a video in your widget
- **Engagement rate:** Percentage of views that resulted in an interaction

### Single video

- **Loads:** Number of times your video was loaded
- **Plays:** Number of times someone started playing your video
- **Play rate:** Percentage of loads that resulted in a play

### Walls of Love

- **Views:** Number of times your Wall of Love was viewed
- **CTA clicks:** Number of times any CTA in your Wall of Love was clicked
- **CTA click rate:** Percentage of views that resulted in a CTA click

## Disable tracking on widgets

Add the `data-track="false"` attribute to your widget's embed code to disable analytics tracking:

```html
<script src="https://widget.senja.io/widget/a2ec3a21-cdad-461e-9192-cee4a15383fe/platform.js" type="text/javascript" async></script>
<div class="senja-embed" data-track="false" data-id="a2ec3a21-cdad-461e-9192-cee4a15383fe" data-mode="shadow" data-lazyload="false" style="display: block;"></div>
```
