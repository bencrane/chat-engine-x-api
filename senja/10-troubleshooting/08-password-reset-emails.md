# What to do when password reset emails aren't received

If you've requested a password reset but haven't received the email, this guide will help you troubleshoot the issue and get back into your account.

Quick ChecklistBefore diving into detailed troubleshooting, try these quick fixes:Check your spam/junk folder - This is the most common solutionWait 10-15 minutes - Email delivery can be delayedVerify your email address - Make sure there are no typosTry a different browser - Clear cache and cookies if neededStep-by-Step Email Troubleshooting
1.

Check All Email FoldersPassword reset emails are frequently flagged as spam.

Check these locations:Spam/Junk folder: Look for emails from Senja or noreply addressesPromotions tab: If you use Gmail, check the Promotions tabAll Mail folder: Search for "Senja" or "password reset"Blocked senders: Make sure Senja emails aren't blocked
2.

Verify Your Email AddressCommon email address issues include:Typos in the email address (extra letters, wrong domain)Using a different email than originally registeredExtra spaces before or after the email addressConfusion between work and personal email addressesTry these email variations:Your work email if you entered personal emailYour personal email if you entered work emailAny other email addresses you commonly use
3.

Email Provider-Specific IssuesSome email providers have stricter filtering:Corporate emails: Company firewalls may block password reset emailsEducational emails (.edu): Often have strict security filtersOutlook/Hotmail: Check the "Other" folder in addition to spamYahoo: Look in both spam and bulk folders
4.

Technical SolutionsClear your browser cache and cookies:This can resolve issues with the password reset formTry requesting the reset in an incognito/private browsing windowTry a different device or browser:Sometimes browser extensions or settings interfereMobile browsers may work when desktop doesn'tDisable browser extensions temporarily:Ad blockers or privacy extensions can interfereTry disabling them and requesting reset againWhen Self-Service Doesn't WorkIf you've tried all the above steps and still haven't received the password reset email, it's time to contact support.
