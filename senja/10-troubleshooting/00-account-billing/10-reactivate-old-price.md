# Can I reactivate my subscription at the old price?

When you reactivate a subscription, it will be at the current pricing.

We don't support old pricing for new or reactivated subscriptions, with one exception for payment failures.

Reactivating your subscriptionReactivated subscriptions use current pricing.

If you previously canceled and want to subscribe again, you'll pay the current plan rates.

Resubscribing after a payment failureIf your subscription was canceled due to a payment failure, you may be able to resubscribe at your old price.

If you were on our Starter plan, you can use the promo code STARTERLEGACY at checkout to keep your old pricing.

If you've already resubscribed at the new price, contact our support team and we'll issue a refund and apply a coupon so you're billed at your old price moving forward.

Need a refund instead?

If you accidentally upgraded to the wrong plan or billing cycle, you may be eligible for a refund.

See our guide on how to get a refund for accidental subscription upgrades.
