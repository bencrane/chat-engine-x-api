# Troubleshooting the Proof module

Some users have reported an issue where the Proof module fails to load, which can prevent the import of testimonials.

This is often caused by a caching issue or a conflict with your browser extensions.

SolutionIf you're experiencing this issue, please try the following troubleshooting steps:Clear your browser cache and cookies.

This is the most common cause of the issue.

Disable all browser extensions.

Some extensions can interfere with the Proof module.

Try a different browser.

This will help you determine if the issue is specific to your browser.

Ensure you are on a stable internet connection.

If you've tried all of the above and are still having trouble, please contact our support team for further assistance.
