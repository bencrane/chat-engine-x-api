# How to restore a deleted form and its testimonials

You can restore a deleted form and its associated testimonials by contacting our support team.

This process is handled by support and is not available as a self-service option.

What happens when a form is deleted?

When you accidentally delete a form, both the form and all testimonials collected through that form are removed from your account.

This can happen when:Clearing your browser cacheAccidentally deleting the wrong form when managing duplicatesBulk operations that affect multiple formsDon't worry - our support team can restore both your form and its testimonials in most cases.

How to request form restorationTo restore your deleted form and testimonials, contact our support team and provide the following information:Required informationForm details: The name or URL of the deleted form (if you remember it)Deletion timeframe: When you deleted the form (approximate date and time)Testimonial details: Names and email addresses of customers who submitted testimonials through this formEmail notifications: Forward any email notifications you received from Senja about testimonials collected through this formAdditional helpful informationScreenshots of the form or testimonials (if available)Description of what happened when the form was deletedYour Senja account email addressRestoration timelineOur support team typically responds to restoration requests within a few hours during business hours.

In most cases, we can restore your form and testimonials the same day you contact us.

The restoration process includes:Recovering the deleted form with all its customizations and settingsRestoring all testimonials that were collected through the formMaintaining the original submission dates and customer informationPrevention tipsTo avoid accidentally deleting forms in the future:Be careful when editing your forms - double-check which form you're working withWhen creating duplicates, clearly name them before deleting the originalAvoid clearing browser cache while working in the form editorConsider using form version history instead of deletion when making major changes Need to recover individual testimonials?

If you only need to restore specific testimonials (not an entire form), check our guide on how to recover a deleted testimonial.

Contact supportReady to restore your form?

Contact our support team with the required information above.

We're here to help get your testimonials back quickly.
