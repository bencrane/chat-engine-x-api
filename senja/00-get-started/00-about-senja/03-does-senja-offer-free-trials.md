# Does Senja offer free trials?

Instead of time-limited free trials, we give a generous free plan that lets you explore our core features without any time pressure.

The free plan includes up to 15 testimonials, imports from 30+ platforms, customizable forms, and access to our widget library with Senja branding.

Why we chose a free plan over trialsA free plan gives you the time to properly test Senja in your workflow without the stress of a ticking clock.
