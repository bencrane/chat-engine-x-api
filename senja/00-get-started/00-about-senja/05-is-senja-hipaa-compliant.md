# Is Senja HIPAA compliant?

Senja is not HIPAA compliant and does not meet the requirements for handling protected health information (PHI).

What this means for healthcare organizationsIf you work in healthcare and need to collect testimonials that might contain patient information, you should not use Senja. HIPAA requires specific security measures and business associate agreements that Senja does not provide.

Alternative approachesConsider these options if you're in healthcare:Collect testimonials that don't mention specific medical conditions or treatmentsUse anonymous testimonials without any identifying patient informationWork with patients to create testimonials that focus on general experience rather than medical detailsIf you have questions about data handling or privacy, please contact our Support team.
