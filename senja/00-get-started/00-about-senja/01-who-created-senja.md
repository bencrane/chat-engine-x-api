# Who created Senja?

Hey, I'm Olly Meakings the cofounder of Senja.

For the first three years I worked alongside Senja's other cofounder Wilson Wilson, but he later decided to leave so that he could launch Ferndesk.

Ferndesk is the software that powers this knowledge base.

How did we meet?

We actually met on X through the #buildinpublic tag, and decided to work together to build and market Senja.

Surprisingly we've still not met in real life, but a plan is being made.

Expect a selfie on this page when we do.
