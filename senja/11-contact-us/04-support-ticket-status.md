# How to check the status of your support ticket

Track your support ticket by replying to the confirmation email you received when you submitted it.

Check your ticket statusWhen you submit a support ticket, you'll receive a confirmation email from our team.

To check the status or request an update:Find the confirmation email in your inboxReply to that email with your question Keep the original confirmation email handy so you can easily reply to it for updates.

What to expectResponse times vary depending on:Complexity of your issueCurrent ticket volume We're experiencing higher than usual ticket volume, which may result in delays.

We're working to respond to everyone as quickly as possible.

Haven't heard back?

Reply to your original confirmation email to ask for an update on your ticket.
