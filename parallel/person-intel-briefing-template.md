# Person Intel Briefing — Parallel Deep Research Template

## Dynamic Variables

From cumulative context / prior steps:
- `{client_company_name}` — the client whose product is being sold (e.g., SecurityPal)
- `{client_company_description}` — what the client sells
- `{customer_company_name}` — the client's customer where this person previously worked (e.g., Snap)
- `{person_full_name}` — the person's full name
- `{person_linkedin_url}` — LinkedIn profile URL
- `{person_current_job_title}` — current title
- `{person_current_company_name}` — where they work now
- `{person_current_company_domain}` — domain of where they work now
- `{person_current_company_description}` — what that company does
