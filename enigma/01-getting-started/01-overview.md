## Getting Started

Enigma is the most reliable, trusted source of data on business in the United States.

Enigma delivers actionable intelligence on the identity and activity of every U.S. business, from the smallest sole proprietorships to the largest multinationals. This data powers industry-leading Business Verification and KYB services, supports data-driven GTM teams with account-based data enrichment, payment volume insights, and leads for sales and marketing campaigns.

Powered by the latest generation of Enigma's entity resolution technology, Enigma `graph-model-1` provides the most comprehensive view of every business' identity, activity, and relationships. Begin using the Enigma Console to start exploring the data interactively or begin using our API to build your own applications and direct data integrations.
