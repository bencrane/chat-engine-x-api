# Enigma + Your AI via MCP

URL: https://documentation.enigma.com/guides/ai-mcp

Beta
The Enigma remote MCP server is currently in beta.

**Model Context Protocol (MCP)** connects your AI to the Enigma comprehensive business intelligence platform. Through MCP, your AI gets instant access to the [Enigma data graph](https://enigma.com/data) and powerful analytics across millions of U.S. businesses.

**Your AI becomes a business intelligence expert** - able to search companies, analyze financial performance, investigate corporate structures, discover competitors, and assess compliance risks in real-time.

## What Your AI Can Do

Through MCP, your AI gains access to the full Enigma business intelligence toolkit. These capabilities work independently or as part of complex reasoning chains:

### Core Business Intelligence

Your AI can:

- **Search any business** - Get comprehensive profiles with revenue data, location count, technologies, and Brand ID
- **Analyze all locations** - Detailed performance metrics and rankings for every business location
- **Access financial analytics** - Deep financial data with monthly revenue breakdowns

### Competitive & Market Analysis

Your AI can:

- **Find comparable businesses** - Generate competitive segments and market comparisons
- **Discover market opportunities** - Identify similar locations and expansion targets

### Compliance & Risk Assessment

Your AI can:

- **Investigate corporate structures** - Legal entities, registrations, and ownership details
- **Search government databases** - Regulatory filings and institutional data sources
- **Verify business identity** - KYB compliance and identity verification
- **Perform risk screening** - Adverse media monitoring and risk assessment

### Sanctions Screening

Your AI can:

- **Screen individuals** - Check customers against OFAC SDN, EU sanctions, UN sanctions, and other watchlists using [screening tools](/guides/ai-mcp/screening-tools)
- **Screen businesses** - Verify organizations against global sanctions lists
- **Manage screening decisions** - Query, filter, and update screening decisions for compliance workflows
- **Investigate sanctioned entities** - Get detailed profiles of entities on sanctions lists
